APC Fellowships for Data Center Efficiency Research
–The fellowships support Carnegie Mellon Ph.D. students with a research focus in the broad areas of data center efficiency, including power, cooling, environmental monitoring, physical security and management, standardized components for creating solutions through modular and mobile configurations,scalable architectures, adaptability, efficient and rapid deployment, and overall cost reductions. The fellowships enable students to continue developing systems that improve the efficiencies of both power and cooling, as well as the overall energy efficiency of data centers and include tuition for one year.
