CMU Core Competencies Initiative

Help students build core skills that distinguish them in work and life

The Core 4

Collaboration & Teamwork

Contribute to and build strong and inclusive teams.

Communication & Presentation

Make deliberate choices in what and how you communicate.

Information & Data Literacy

Power your ideas through data.

Intercultural & Global Literacy

Explore and integrate diverse cultures and perspectives.

Core competencies are a set of essential capabilities that are important for success across many disciplines and professions. Helping our teaching and learning community provide opportunities for students to enhance and track their development in several core competencies is at the heart of this initiative. When students graduate from CMU, their enhanced abilities in these core competency areas will complement their disciplinary expertise and set them up for further success.

Contact eberly-assist@andrew.cmu.edu for help with identifying, implementing, and/or measuring core competencies for your context.
