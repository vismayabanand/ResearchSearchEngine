Undergraduate Research

TURNING TEXTBOOK KNOWLEDGE
INTO LIFELONG PURPOSE

More than half of BCOE
undergraduate students
conduct research in labs

Research opportunities are available to all undergraduate students. The benefit of applying knowledge from the classroom to the workspace; working alongside peers, graduate students, and faculty; and honing communication and collaboration skills to be used after graduation, can have a life-long impact on a student's success.

Internships

Many engineering students secure internships at local, regional, national, and international companies and organizations during summer and winter breaks. Learn more about internship opportunities at the UCR Career Center.
