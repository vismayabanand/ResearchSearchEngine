News Notes

    • 5/5/2025

      On April 25 CS PhD student William Sentosa won the Community Award for his paper "CellReplay: Towards accurate record-and-replay for cellular networks"at  USENIX NSDI 2025 on April 29, 2025 in Philadelphia, PA. His advisor, CS professor Brighten Godfrey, is a co-author.

    • 5/5/2025

      "Software Engineering meets Control Theory," a paper from CS professor Sasa Misailovic and collaborators, received a 10-year most influential paper at the SEAMS 2025 Conference in Ottawa, Canada.

    • 4/9/2025

      CS professors Lawrence Angrave, David M. Dalpiaz, Hongye Liu, and lead author Pablo D. Robles Granda received a second-place award for the paper "Beyond Descriptive Statistics: Analysis of Time Series of Student Interactions in Engineering Courses Through the Lens of Modern Mathematical Methods” in the ASEE New Engineering Educators division. It is under consideration for a Best Paper Award.
