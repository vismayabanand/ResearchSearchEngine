General Department Resources

  • CS Department Calendar
  • Add CSD events to your Google calendar:
    • Open Google calendar in a browser
    • Click the ＋ symbol next to Other calendars in the left sidebar
    • Select From URL from the menu that appears
    • In the dialogue that opens, paste the dept. URL: https://csd.cmu.edu/ical/ical.ics
    • Do not select Import - that will irreversibly copy the events into one of your existing calendars instead.
    • Do not select Subscribe to Calendar - this seems to only work properly if it is another Google calendar.
