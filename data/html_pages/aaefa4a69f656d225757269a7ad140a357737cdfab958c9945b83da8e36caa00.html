About UCLA Fire

UCLA Fire (FDID 19945), is a California state fire agency pursuant to Health and Safety Code § 13146, with authority over all UCLA-owned and -occupied interests, both on and off campus. Its Sworn Peace Officers respond to calls for emergency service, mitigate threats/hazards to the University, prepare the public to manage emergencies, partner with stakeholders to respond and recover from emergencies and disasters, investigate fire and explosion incidents, and enforce California fire and explosion law.
