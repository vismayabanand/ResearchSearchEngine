Microsoft Research Ph.D. Fellowships
– Microsoft Research annually recognizes Ph.D. students who represent the best and the brightest from North America. The two-year fellowship covers their tuition and fees and includes a travel allowance, the offer of a paid internship, and an annual stipend.
