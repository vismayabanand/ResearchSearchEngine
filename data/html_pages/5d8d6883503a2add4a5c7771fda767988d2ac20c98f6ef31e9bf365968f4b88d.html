Search settings

  • Limit your search to scholarly content. Includes University presses.

  • Stanford subscribes to the full text, or it's available via open access.

How do I access articles?

On campus

No configuration required. Just click the links in the search results or detail page.

Off campus

You’ll need to log in. You can log in now, or when you find an article of interest.

Search tip

Enter your search in lower case or mixed case, never in ALL CAPS.
Some upper-case words in your search may be interpreted as boolean or field codes and will cause the search to fail.
