Welcome Transfer Students, to the Bourns College of Engineering at UC Riverside!

 

The Marlan and Rosemary Bourns College of Engineering (BCOE) Transfer Student Center provides a safe and inclusive environment.
We dedicate to assisting transfer students with adjusting to the university and engineering.

 

At the center, located in WCH 103, you’ll:

  • Find your community in a home away from home
  • Receive access to important adaptive resources
  • Establish connections with faculty members and engineering peers
