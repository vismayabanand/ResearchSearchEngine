Capital One Book Scholarship
--The Capital One Book Scholarship Award is granted each spring and fall semester to four undergraduates in the School of Computer Science. The award recognizes academic accomplishment and the work assignments students must commit to in order to realize their goals. Capital One is delighted to honor these deserving students.
