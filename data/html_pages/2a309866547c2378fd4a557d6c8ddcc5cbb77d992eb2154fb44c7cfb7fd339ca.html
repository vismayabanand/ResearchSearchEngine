UCLA Events & Destinations

Discover the vibrant tapestry of public events and cultural experiences UCLA has to offer in Los Angeles! This resource highlights an array of lectures, performances, exhibitions and enriching activities hosted by UCLA, showcasing our commitment to fostering creativity, dialogue and community engagement. Whether your passion lies in the arts, academia, or local happenings, this platform connects you to the incredible cultural offerings that UCLA brings to life in the city and beyond. Join us and experience the excitement firsthand!

Get ready for an unforgettable night of music, talent, and Bruin pride at UCLA’s Spring Sing! Join us as students light up the stage with incredible performances and celebrate one of UCLA’s most cherished traditions.

Julio Frenk's 4 Operas Everyone Should See

Meet Julio Frenk, UCLA’s new chancellor on the lookout to change the landscape of higher education. Chancellor Frenk is bringing in a new era, and with it his passions for service and art. One thing you may not know about him… he is an opera buff extraordinaire. You can see one of his bonafide recommendations, Ainadamar, at the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion in downtown Los Angeles this April and May. You can also learn more about Chancellor Frenk’s passion for opera, the books that have most influenced his life and his plans for UCLA in the Winter 2025 issue of UCLA Magazine.

Destinations

UCLA is home to an array of vibrant destinations that enrich the campus experience and invite exploration. Whether you’re seeking artistic expression, live performances or a peaceful escape, UCLA’s destinations offer a unique blend of culture, creativity and tranquility for everyone to enjoy. Dive into the dynamic spirit of our campus and discover a world of inspiration waiting at every turn.

Hammer Museum

Discover the cultural heartbeat of Los Angeles at the Hammer Museum, where UCLA brings the arts to life. Discover an eclectic array of thought-provoking exhibitions, engaging talks, and dynamic events that reflect the diverse voices and cutting-edge creativity of our community.

UCLA Nimoy Theater

Step into the Nimoy Theater at UCLA and embrace the power of storytelling through film, lectures, and performances. Located just steps from the Hammer Museum in the heart of Los Angeles, this intimate space provides a platform for contemporary artists from around the globe to showcase new and diverse works.

Mathias Botanical Garden

Visit our lush 7.5 acre oasis nestled within UCLA campus - an inviting public garden, outdoor classroom, and research hub. Specializing in curated collections that showcase botanical diversity, emphasizing biogeography, conservation, and natural history - our garden promotes community well-being and provides a tranquil space for all to enjoy.
