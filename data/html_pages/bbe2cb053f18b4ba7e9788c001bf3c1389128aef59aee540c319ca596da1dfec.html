The Special Patient Care Clinic (SPCC), formerly known as the Hospital Dentistry, provides advanced comprehensive general dentistry for patients with severe medical, physical, and mental concerns (pre and post-organ transplant patients, cancer patients, immunocompromised patients, special needs patients). 

Dental services offered:

  • Hospital Dentistry
  • General Dentistry
  • IV Sedation in clinic or operating room
  • Emergency Services at UCLA Ronald Reagan Medical Center

Social Work at SPCC 
The Department of Social Welfare and SPCC at UCLA are collaborating to re-imagine patient-centered care. Our goal is to meet patients where they are and to increase social support as individuals navigate the healthcare system. 

We offer the support of a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) and Social Work Graduate Student at SPCC. Social work can be contacted at (310) 206-6670 or email:

New Patients and Payment Information

Due to high interest and lack of providers in the community for people with special needs and medical complexities, we have a waitlist of 12-18 months to become a new patient. 

All new patients must attend an initial screening appointment to be accepted into our clinic. No dental treatment is provided at this appointment.

  • Please fill out our New Patient Request Form to be added to our waitlist


Insurances Accepted:  
Denti-Cal, Delta Dental PPO, Private Pay. If you are not sure what kind of Medi-Cal Dental Insurance (Denti-Cal) you have, we can verify for you when your appointment is scheduled.

Need to Make, Cancel or Reschedule and Appointment?

For questions regarding appointments, billing, or your care, please contact (310) 825-6510.
