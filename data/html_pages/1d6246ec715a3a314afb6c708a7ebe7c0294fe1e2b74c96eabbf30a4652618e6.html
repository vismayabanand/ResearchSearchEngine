The Stanford Pioneers Pledge

The entrepreneurial spirit of Stanford University is one of the most treasured features of our campus culture.

Skip to Section

Our graduates are behind many successful startups, and exploration and innovation are part of the Stanford ethos. Signing the Stanford Pioneers Pledge means joining an exceptional group of entrepreneurs, founding employees, and other innovators who are signaling their support for the university.

By incorporating philanthropy into your entrepreneurship, you can help the university grow alongside your own endeavors — and inspire others to do the same. 

Frequently asked questions

  • The Pioneers Pledge is a non-binding agreement that signals your intent to support the university through your business success. It signals your commitment to Stanford and inspires others to do the same.

  • We welcome anyone who considers themselves a pioneer: entrepreneurs, founders, early employees, other innovators. You do not have to be an alumnus of the university.

  • No. Signing the pledge does not require a specific dollar amount or equity percentage commitment. When the time is right, you can make a gift that feels significant to you. We’ll provide more information about any applicable gift policies at that time.

    You also don’t need to decide how you want to designate your pledge until you make a gift. This way, we can help you explore areas of interest across Stanford and help you achieve your philanthropic goals.

  • There are no tax implications until you sign a gift agreement. At that time, we can help you figure out the best way to move forward, depending on your unique circumstances. 

  • You become part of a special community of entrepreneurs who are not only building some of the world’s most innovative businesses, but also care deeply about Stanford. You’ll receive information about events and research that we believe would be of interest to this community, and you’ll have a point of contact who can help you discover new interests and connect with your fellow pioneers.

  • If you’ve already supported the university through an exit or other wealth event, we want to recognize you! Please reach out to pioneerspledge@stanford.edu.

  • We would love to hear from you! Email us at pioneerspledge@stanford.edu.

Contact us

Bring us your questions about Pioneers Pledge or start your pledge process. We are excited to speak with you.
