Travel Information & Resources

During your time at CMU, you may wish to travel - outside the U.S. or domestically - to visit someone during a break, to take a vacation, or to return to your home country. But before you pack your suitcase, it is important to be prepared so you can maintain your legal immigration status and return to the U.S. as expected. 

This section of the OIE website contains up-to-date travel information, tips and advice. Remember - it is a good idea to consult with OIE before any travel to ensure you do not encounter any issues when re-entering the U.S. and returning to Carnegie Mellon.

Quick Links:

  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Automatic Visa Revalidation
  • Foreign Embassies in the U.S.

Important Travel Note:

Students and scholars should carefully consider entry requirements or restrictions for any country you may be traveling to or through before finalizing your travel plans. Keep in mind that requirements may also change while you are traveling and/or abroad. Please note that OIE advisors can only advise on re-entry to the U.S.
