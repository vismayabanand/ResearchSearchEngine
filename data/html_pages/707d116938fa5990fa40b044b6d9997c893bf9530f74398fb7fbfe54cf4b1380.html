Tuition & Fees

*The information below applies to Stanford's Honors Cooperative Program, in which you can earn a part-time master’s degree. This program is administered through the Stanford Engineering Center for Global & Online Education (formerly Stanford Center for Professional Development).

Tuition

Tuition for each course is $1,514 per unit. Each course ranges from 3-5 units, as indicated on course pages. You must earn 45 units of credit within five years of your start date to be awarded a Master’s degree.

At current tuition, the approximate cost of a master's degree through the Honors Cooperative Program is $70,000, inclusive of fees.

Total UnitsQuarterly Tuition (unless otherwise specified)
Total Units 1-7Quarterly Tuition (unless otherwise specified) $1,514 per unit
Total Units 8-10Quarterly Tuition (unless otherwise specified) $14,100
Total Units 11-18Quarterly Tuition (unless otherwise specified) $21,694

Additional required fees

  • The document fee for matriculated students admitted to a new degree is $250 and for non-matriculated students in non-degree programs is $125. (not applicable if you already paid this fee for a credit-bearing graduate course prior to starting your degree program).
  • Associated Students of Stanford University (ASSU) Fee, assessed each autumn, winter, or spring quarter you are enrolled. Learn more at the Student Financial Services webpage under the "Tuition and Other Fees Schedules" section.

Note: All amounts are shown in USD.

Military benefits

You may be eligible to receive tuition benefits through your VA education benefit. For more information, please review our VA Education Benefits page.

Financial Aid

Contact the Stanford University Financial Aid office for more information.

Payment

Please expect to receive your enrollment confirmation within seven days after the quarter’s final study list deadline.

After the final study list deadline has passed and your quarterly enrollment is processed by our team, you will receive a notification to log into your mystanfordconnection account to arrange payment. Payment for quarterly tuition and fees is due upon receipt of enrollment notification. Tuition and fees must be paid in full; installment payments are not allowed. Payment may be made by credit card, wire transfer, direct invoice to a company, purchase order, company voucher, letter of credit, company check, or Yellow Ribbon tuition benefits (for veterans).

If you pay using a credit card, a receipt will automatically be sent to you by email. The receipt will be sent to the preferred email address listed in your mystanfordconnection account. If you need a receipt at a later date, please contact us at stanfordonline-gradprograms@stanford.edu.

If your company has agreed to pay for your tuition, our finance department will send an invoice to your company within seven days after the quarter’s final study list deadline. Late payment fees will be charged to the student’s account, unless noted otherwise.

Tuition and fees payable to the Stanford Engineering Center for Global & Online Education include:

  • Tuition per credit unit (3 unit minimum)
  • One-time document fee (only charged for the first quarter registered at Stanford)
  • ASSU (Associated Students of Stanford University) fee for local HCP students

All other fees, such as housing, health insurance, and petition fees to change course enrollment after the deadline must be paid directly to the university through Student Financial Services.

Payment Policy

Both the university and CGOE tuition and fees are subject to annual review and may be adjusted. All changes will take effect at the start of autumn quarter and are applicable for the duration of the academic year.

Tuition Refund Policy

Before the Financial Add/Drop Deadline, all tuition paid to the Stanford Engineering Center for Global & Online Education for courses dropped will be refunded in full, less any late fees that are assessed. 

After the Financial Add/Drop Deadline, tuition and fees paid to the Stanford Engineering Center for Global & Online Education will not be subject to refund except under the following circumstances:

1. Medical emergency (hospitalization or major medical condition of the student). Official written and signed documentation from a physician will be required (which notes date originally seen, diagnosis and prognosis).

2. Death of a “close family” member. “Close family” is limited to the student’s spouse, domestic partner, children, parents and parents-in-law, parent surrogate, brothers and sisters, grandparents and grandchildren. Written documentation will be required (Death Certificate).

3. A call to active military duty (in the event of a national emergency such as in a time of war). Written documentation will be required.

4. Leave of Absence - If you are approved for leave of absence before the start of the quarter, you are eligible to receive a full tuition refund.

If you are approved for leave of absence before the withdrawal deadline, you are eligible to receive a prorated refund based on your days of attendance. CGOE will invoice you after the per diem tuition fee appears in your Axess account. Please pay through your mystanfordconnection account, not Axess. There is no tuition refund for leaves taken after the Term Withdrawal deadline.

For more information, please review Stanford's deadlines and refund policy for leaves of absence.

Refunds

If you are due a refund and your original payment was made by credit card, that same credit card account will be credited within 5 business days. If you made payment via wire transfer, a check will be issued as a refund within 10 business days of the wire transfer clearing. If the payment was made by company check, the company will be granted a refund by check.
