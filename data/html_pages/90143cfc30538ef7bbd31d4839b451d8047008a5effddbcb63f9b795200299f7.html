Getting Started 

The Center for Scholarships & Scholar Enrichment (formerly Scholarship Resource Center) helps UCLA students identify and apply for scholarships. We offer workshops, individual counseling, and other resources. We are separate from the Financial Aid Office. Please see our FAQ here. 

We offer all of our services virtually, and we are available in person for questions. Our office is open Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays in person; and  Mondays and Fridays through Zoom virtually.

Please go to our Services and Events pages for more details about how to register for Zoom advising sessions.
