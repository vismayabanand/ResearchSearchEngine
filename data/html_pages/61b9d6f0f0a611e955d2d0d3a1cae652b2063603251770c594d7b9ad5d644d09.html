Incoming Graduate Students and Postdocs

Welcome to Carnegie Mellon University! As a new graduate student or postdoc, you may have a broad range of questions. The Office of Graduate and Postdoctoral Affairs is here to support you as you transition to life as a graduate student or postdoc.

Please access the appropriate section below to review some of the actions items you should take upon beginning your CMU journey as a graduate student or postdoc.

Incoming Postdoc Action Items

In addition to exploring the OGPA website, postdocs should consult their department website and the Human Resources website for additional resources. Supervisors may also provide additional information to postdocs as they transition to the Carnegie Mellon community.
