Microsoft Imagine Cup
–The Microsoft Imagine Cup is among the world's premier student technology competitions. Eligible students are invited to use their imagination and passion to create a technology solution that addresses the Imagine Cup theme:for 2012, imagine a world where technology helps to solve the problems posed by the U.N. Millenium Goals. Since its inception in 2003, the Imagine Cup has grown into a global competition focused on finding solutions to real-world problems. Since its start, over 1.4 million students have participated, with 358,000 students representing 183 countries and regions represented in the competition.
