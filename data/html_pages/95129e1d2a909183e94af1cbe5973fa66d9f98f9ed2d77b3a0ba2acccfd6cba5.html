R&DE Divisions

Senior Associate Vice Provost

The Senior Associate Vice Provost leads Stanford’s largest auxiliary organization and is an essential advisor for many other university initiatives.

Student Housing Operations

R&DE Student Housing Operations offers a variety of  housing options for undergraduate and graduate students and their dependents.

Stanford Dining, Hospitality & Auxiliaries

R&DE Stanford Dining, Hospitality & Auxiliaries provides high-quality, nutritious cuisine to meet the wide variety of dietary needs within our diverse Stanford community.

Stanford Conferences

Stanford Conferences provides expertise to support your conference or meeting –  including in-person, virtual and hybrid events – including support for Stanford summer camps and conferences for adults and children.

Maintenance Operations & Capital Projects

MCP leads R&DE stewardship with major renovations and routine maintenance in addition to providing leadership in sustainability and emergency preparedness.
