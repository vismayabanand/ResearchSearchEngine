Core@CMU

The inaugural offering of Core@CMU launched in Fall 2024.

Core@CMU is an engaging, online learning experience where students develop core competency skills in the "Core 4" domains:

  • Collaboration & Teamwork
  • Communication & Presentation
  • Information & Data Literacy
  • Intercultural & Global Literacy

Additionally, students discover evidence-based strategies to enhance their learning. This foundational course is an essential step in students' educational journey at CMU, setting them up for academic, personal, and professional success across any field of study.
