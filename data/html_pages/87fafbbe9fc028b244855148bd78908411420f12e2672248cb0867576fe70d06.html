Arts Pass

All graduate and undergraduate CMU students can gain free access to eight Pittsburgh venues with their Carnegie Mellon ID. In order to be eligible, you must be enrolled in classes and your primary affiliation must be “student”.

At this time, faculty, staff, and sponsored guests are not eligible for the Art Pass program.  

Please review the information below and check the museums' websites before planning your trip to learn the most up-to-date information regarding their current hours of operation and ticketing procedures.

Planning Your Visit

Carnegie Museum of Natural History, Carnegie Museum of Art, The Andy Warhol Museum & Carnegie Science Center

Free general admission is available for CMU students with a valid student ID. Students will be asked to present their student ID card upon entering the museum.

All faculty-led groups and docent-led tours must be pre-registered by reaching out to each museum’s respective program registration office.

  • Carnegie Museums of Art and Natural History | 412-622-3131 | oakadmissions@carnegiemuseums.org
  • Carnegie Science Center | 412-237-3400 | CustomerService@carnegiesciencecenter.org
  • The Andy Warhol Museum | 412-237-8300 | information@warhol.org
  • Visit the Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh website for more information.

Mattress Factory

Free admission for CMU students is available on a walk-up basis. CMU students are asked not to purchase their ticket online; instead, they should check in at the admissions desk upon arrival. Please bring your valid CMU student ID with you to gain free entry to Mattress Factory.

Visit the Mattress Factory website for more information.

National Aviary

Free admission for CMU students is available for visitors on a walk-up basis. CMU students are asked not to purchase a timed ticket but to present their CMU ID card to museum staff upon arrival. Please bring your valid student ID with you for your discount and entry.

Visit the National Aviary website for more information.

Phipps Botanical Gardens

CMU students may visit Phipps during their normal business hours without prior booking. Please have your student ID ready when you approach the admissions desk. CMU students who wish to book their tickets in advance may do so by emailing tickets@phipps.conservatory.org or calling 412-622-6914. Please bring your valid student ID card when visiting Phipps

Visit the Phipps ticketing website for more information.

Heinz History Center, Fort Pitt Museum & Meadowcroft Rockshelter

Free admission for CMU students is available for visitors on a walk-up basis. CMU students are asked not to purchase a timed ticket but to present their CMU ID card to museum staff upon arrival. Please bring your valid student ID with you for your discount and entry.

Visit the Heinz History Center website for more information.
