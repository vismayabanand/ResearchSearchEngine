    MIT hosts a tremendous range of committees and groups dedicated to advancing the Institutes objectives and goals for diversity, equity, and inclusion. Participants in the groups below, which are all organized around campus-wide mandates or charters, are drawn from academic, research, and administrative units all across the MIT.

    For more detailed listings of active committees at MIT, please consult the individual listings on our Local Resources and Contacts page.
