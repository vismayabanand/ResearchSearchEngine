Assistant Vice Provost's Message

Welcome to Stanford Dining, Hospitality and Auxiliaries

Stanford Dining, Hospitality & Auxiliaries takes immense pride in nourishing the Stanford community through exceptional culinary experiences, fostering a strong sense of community and enriching students' academic journey. We stand at the forefront of culinary excellence, sustainability, and education, and promoting responsible food choices that empower students to become mindful citizens who can positively impact the world."

 

Eric Montell, Assistant Vice Provost

Culinary Excellence

Our commitment to Culinary Excellence creates a high-quality dining experience that promotes well-being, fosters community, and builds lasting memories through delicious, safe, sustainable, and nutritious food.

Discover Culinary Excellence
Supporting the Stanford Community

We support the Stanford community through various initiatives, such as the BeWell Community gardens, monthly pop-up food pantries, and using proceeds from R&DE retail locations to offset room and board increases and support student programs.

See Our Impact

Stanford Food Institute

The Stanford Food Institute unites a diverse community to advance research, education, and policy for a better food future, emphasizing collaboration and innovation for nourishing, sustainable, and equitable solutions.
