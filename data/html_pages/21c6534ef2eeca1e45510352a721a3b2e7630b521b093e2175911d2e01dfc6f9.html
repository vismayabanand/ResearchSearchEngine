Electronic Data Systems Book Scholarship
--The EDS Academic Excellence Award is granted each spring and fall semester to six students from the School of Computer Science. The award recognizes scholastic accomplishment and overall departmental involvement. The award includes an EDS Book Scholarship in the amount of $300. EDS is proud to honor these deserving students as part of its Campus Relations program.
