Our Mission

El Centro Chicano y Latino supports all undergraduate and graduate students throughout their journey at Stanford. El Centro nourishes the academic, professional, sociopolitical, cultural, and personal well-being of our communities. Since its establishment in 1978, we have advocated for equity, justice, and accessibility, particularly in issues affecting the Latin American diaspora. We empower scholars, community builders, and advocates to lead with excellence, integrity, and social responsibility.  El Centro fosters a sense of belonging and builds connections across Stanford and beyond.
