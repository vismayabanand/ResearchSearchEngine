Admitted Students – You Did It!

Congratulations to everyone who's been admitted to the University of California, Riverside (UCR)! We welcome your bold heart and brilliant mind. Ranked in the top 15 for best public universities in the nation, UCR has created a new model for what a great public research university can help you achieve!

Visit the MyUCR applicant portal to check your admission status. After that, we’ll show you how to say “YES” to UCR, take your next steps to enrollment, and join our welcoming and diverse community of Highlanders who are propelling ideas forward and transforming lives across the country and around the world.
