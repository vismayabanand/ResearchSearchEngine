Master's Degrees

Accomplish your goals with a master’s degree from Stanford. Our online and part-time programs, taught by Stanford faculty, focus on developing deep expertise, building skill sets, and advancing careers without interrupting them.

Spotlight

Celebrating 70 years: Stanford Honors Cooperative Program

For 70 years, the School of Engineering has been seeding innovation through an education exchange between academic thought leaders and industry professionals. Stanford’s Honors Cooperative Program (HCP) provides working professionals worldwide the opportunity to pursue a master’s degree on a part-time basis.

  2. Review the admissions and enrollment periods.
