Undergraduate Programs
Discover your options


University of California, Riverside’s (UCR) School of Business operates the largest undergraduate business program — and the only actuarial science degree program in a business school — within the University of California system. In addition to seven concentrations for the business administration major, the School of Business offers a business minor. These offerings make us one of the best business schools in California.

Graduate Programs
Explore the programs


You can earn a STEM-designated business master’s degree from one of the best business schools in the nation. We offer an MBA, Professional MBA, Master of Finance (MFin), Master of Professional Accountancy (MPAc), and Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA). You can also enter our Ph.D. program and earn your doctorate. A STEM-designated master’s in business can boost your career trajectory.

Why Choose UCR’s School of Business?

Rigorous STEM-Designated Programs
>>
With a STEM-designated master’s in business degree from one of the best business schools in California, you demonstrate to employers that you possess the combination of technical skills and business acumen necessary to add significant value to their organization.

If you are an international student seeking a UCR business master’s degree, the STEM designation enables you to work in the United States for up to three years of Optional Practical Training (OPT) after graduation.
Intelligently Inclusive Campus
>>
UCR is one of the most diverse campuses in California, which makes us one of the best business schools in the nation for all students. Nearly 60% of our undergraduate population are first-generation college students and approximately 40% of our domestic graduate student population come from underrepresented racial/ethnic backgrounds. Additionally, nearly half of our students identify as women.
 
Our students learn alongside others from all economic levels, racial and ethnic backgrounds, genders, work experiences, and educational backgrounds. Your interactions in and out of classroom settings help you develop unique perspectives, leading to rich discussions and impactful solutions.
 Dedicated Faculty and Staff
>>
UCR faculty members are not just accomplished researchers and practitioners ― they are subject matter experts, effective teachers, and mentors who care about your success. Our professional staff members provide caring support throughout your educational journey — making us one of the best business schools in California to jumpstart your career.
Undergraduate and Graduate Degree Programs >>
Our B.S. in business administration and our newest B.S. in actuarial science teach you the skills you need to thrive in business. Our business master’s degrees prepare you to become a leader in your field. At UCR, you will develop the knowledge and skills you need to make a difference in the world. You will work on real-world projects and network with business leaders who may offer job opportunities or internships.

UCR School of Business Highlights

New School of Business Building
>>
Undergraduate and graduate business degree candidates at UCR welcomed a new building mid October 2024. The new LEED-certified building is designed to adapt to uses and technology yet unknown and includes indoor and outdoor in-person learning, studying, event, and meeting spaces. The flagship facility serves as the new home of UCR’s School of Business.
