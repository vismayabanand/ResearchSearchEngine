Master of Software Engineering Programs

Software and Societal Systems Department

For Applicants

Our world-renowned Master of Software Engineering degree will equip you with the tools and techniques necessary to design and build the massive software systems that drive our modern world.

For Companies

From project sponsorship to recruiting graduates, we are committed to working hand-in-hand with industry to build relationships that will take your organization's efforts to the next level.

For Alumni

Our alumni are our greatest asset and pride. From countless nations and backgrounds, our graduates are making an impact on software across domains, verticals, and the globe.  

We are the Master of Software Engineering Professional Programs at Carnegie Mellon University. And we are dedicated to training the next generation of technology leaders. We strive to produce some of the world's best software engineers — those who don't just deliver the next revolutionary software product but those who can, in the process, begin to shape and guide the industry itself as lifelong Agents of Change.
