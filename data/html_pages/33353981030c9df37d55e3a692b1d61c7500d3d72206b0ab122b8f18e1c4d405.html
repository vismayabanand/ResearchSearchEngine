At UCLA, optimism isn’t just a state of mind—it’s a driving force behind everything we do. It fuels our pursuit of excellence, our commitment to innovation and our dedication to making a positive impact on the world. Optimism is more than just a positive attitude; it’s a powerful catalyst for change. It’s about seeing opportunities where others see obstacles and believing in the potential for progress even in the face of challenges. Our UCLA community embodies this spirit every day, turning vision into reality and dreams into achievements.

Here, we celebrate the spirit of optimism that our students, alumni, faculty and donors embody, showcasing how their hopeful outlook and relentless drive are shaping a brighter future for all.
