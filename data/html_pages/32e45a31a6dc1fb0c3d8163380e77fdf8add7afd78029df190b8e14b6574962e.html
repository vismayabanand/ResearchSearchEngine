Kickstart Your Career in Computer Engineering 

Earn your Bachelor’s in Computer Engineering at America’s fastest rising university! 

Work with leaders in the field to create your own computers, video games, or to build and program a robot from scratch. As a CEN undergraduate you will gain the knowledge and skill needed to build and improve computing systems – both hardware and software – ranging from small scale embedded devices to data centers and supercomputers. With numerous electives, you can design the educational path that best fits your interests. And our professional student advisors will help you link your academic knowledge with practical experience. Learn more about our curriculum.
