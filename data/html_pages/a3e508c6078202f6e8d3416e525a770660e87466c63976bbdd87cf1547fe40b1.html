EECSIS Portals

The EECS Information System (EECSIS) is a collection of web pages and scripts to support students, faculty, and headquarters staff.

§ indicates web pages requiring authentication via Touchstone.

General Information

  • Archive of Who's Teaching What and catalog updates by term.
  • Who's Taken What tables showing most-taken subjects by major and year.
  • Degree requirements for EECS undergraduate majors and MEng.

Undergraduate/MEng Students

  • Degree Checklist§ including both GIR and department audits. Petition for substitutions to degree requirements.
  • MEng program: application§, request entry§, submit thesis proposal§, submit thesis§, request extension§.
  • International: request departmental letters for CPT§, OPT§, ISO extension§.
  • 6A program: apply for internship§, submit resume for Resume Book.
  • The Minor in CS§: apply for the minor, review current status.
  • Submit research reports§ for 6.UR and 6.9800.
  • Submit internship completion reports§ for 6.9820/6.9830.
  • Submit appointment reports§ for 6.S956.
  • Submit proposals for IAP§ subjects.

Doctoral Students

  • PhD Status§ including degree milestones and transcript. Submit TQE plan, RQE application, Minor program, Professional Perspective plans and reports, Masters/EE/ECS/Doctoral theses.
  • Submit requested documentation when nominated for fellowships§.

Faculty

  • Faculty records§ including teaching assignments, theses supervised, and sabbatical leaves. Submit request for future sabbatical leave.
  • List of ugrad/MEng§ advisees with links to each student's degree checklist. Advisee photos. Pending subsitution petitions requiring advisor input
  • List of doctoral§ academic and research advisees with links to each student's status page. Advisee photos.
  • Submit annual report§ of research and teaching activities to the deparment.
  • Nominate students for fellowships§.
  • Submit/edit current research interests§.
  • Submit Chair Report for RQE§.

Undergraduate/MEng Office

  • List of current undergraduate and MEng students§ with links to each student's checklist page.
  • List of degree candidates§ with audit of degree requirements.
  • To-do list§ for students needing action by the Undergraduate Office.
  • Manage ugrad/MEng advising assignment§.
  • International: manage departmental letter requests for CPT§, OPT§, ISO extension§.
  • Manage research reports§ for 6.UR and 6.9800.
  • Manage internship completion reports§ for 6.9820/6.9830.
  • List of student advisors§ for ugrad, MEng, and doctoral programs. Set advising quotas for each advisor. Can also be accessed by faculty assistants for advisee lists.
  • The Minor in CS§: review student status, approve completion of the minor.
  • Manage end_of_term§ communications for students with CAP Flags. Preliminary list§ of students to be flagged based on term transcript.
  • View class lists, download grade sheet compatible Registrar's online grade system.

Graduate Office

  • List of current dual-SM and doctoral students§ with links to each student's status page.
  • List of degree candidates§ with audit of degree requirements.
  • To-do list§ for students needing action by the Graduate Office.
  • Manage applications for fellowships§.
  • Support for graduate admissions§.
  • View class lists, download grade sheet compatible Registrar's online grade system.

Headquarters

  • EECS Dashboard§: tables showing number of students by year and major, degree awarded, subject enrollments, etc.
  • List of graduate appointments§ including appointment requests for upcoming terms.
  • View class lists, download grade sheet compatible Registrar's online grade system.
  • DEI Dashboard§: tables for DEI statistics for entering and continuing students, diversity gap in subject grading.
  • Review faculty annual reports§.
  • Process sabbatical requests§.
  • Review subject proposals for IAP§.
  • 6A program: 6A portal§ where you can create internship applications and review submissions, manage Resume Book.
  • Super UROP: dashboard§.
  • Audit check: review which requirements are satisfied by each subject (and vice versa).
  • Class list for specifed subject.
