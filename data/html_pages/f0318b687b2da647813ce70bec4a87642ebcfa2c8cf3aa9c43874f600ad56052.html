Stanford Center for AI Safety Annual Meeting

Event Details:

Wednesday, August 16, 2023
8:30am - 4:35pm PDT

Stanford AI for Safety 2023 Annual Meeting

We invite our industrial partners to the AI Safety Annual Meeting to learn about the latest developments in timely and critical areas of technology. It will be a day filled with informative presentations and networking opportunities in the grand setting of Stanford's Paul Brest Hall. Catered breakfast, lunch and snacks will be provided for your enjoyment.

Date & Time: August 16, 2023 | 8:30am - 4:35pm. This event is being held in-person & online

Location
Paul Brest Hall
555 Salvatierra Walk
Stanford, CA 94035

 

Agenda

8:30 - 9:00 amBreakfast    
9:00 - 9:15 amOpening RemarksAnthony Corso  YouTube
9:15 - 9:45 amSafe and Robust Autonomous Vehicle Localization using AIGrace Gao   
9:45 - 10:15 amTowards Safety and Generalizability for Autonomous Systems Under UncertaintyEsen Yel  YouTube
10:15 - 10:45 amCoffee Break    
10:45 - 11:15 amOn Trustworthy AI and Foundation ModelsSanmi Koyejo  YouTube
11:15 - 11:45 amResponsible Reinforcement LearningEmma Brunskill  YouTube
11:45am - 12:00 pmStudent Flash Talks   YouTube
12:00 - 1:45 pmPoster Session & Lunch    
1:45 - 3:00 pmIndustry Panel: AI Safety in AviationModerator: Esen Yel, Stanford University Panelists: Eric Mueller, Joby Aviation | Louis Dressel, Wing | Arthur Dubois, Xwing | Matt Nehrenz, Reliable Robotics CorporationYouTube
3:00 - 3:30 pmBreak    
3:30 - 4:00 pmVeriX: Towards Verified Explainability of Deep Neural NetworksClark Barrett  YouTube
4:00 - 4:30 pmResearch Updates from the Stanford Intelligent Systems LabMykel Kochenderfer  YouTube
4:30 - 4:35 pmClosing RemarksAnthony Corso  YouTube
