CNAS Undergraduate Academic Advising Center

Welcome to CNAS Advising

Welcome to the CNAS Undergraduate Academic Advising Center (UAAC)! From orientation to graduation, Academic Advisors play a crucial role in helping you achieve your academic goals.  We are committed to offering you academic guidance every step of the way throughout your undergraduate career.  CNAS UAAC offers hybrid appointment options and general drop-in advising in person through our General Undergraduate Advising Check-In's (GUAC) option.  We encourage you to make an appointment through our online scheduling system MyAdvising.ucr.edu or stop by to see us in person at 1223 Pierce Hall.

 

HOW TO ACCESS ADVISING SERVICES

To access or contact your academic advisor, see information below:

  • Visit your major's advising page to find your advisor's contact information and availability.

  • You can schedule an appointment with your advisor, either online or in-person, through MyAdvising.ucr.edu

  • Need quick help with a general advising question? Simply stop by the Advising Center at 1223 Pierce Hall in person for a General Undergrad Advising Check-In (GUAC).

Who to Meet

Welcome, UCR students! When it comes to academic support, it is essential to know who to meet. Upon admission to UCR, you were assigned a dedicated advisor who is here to assist you on your academic journey. To find your assigned advisor, you have two options: check your Student Profile on R'web or visit your CNAS Major page. Learn more about meeting with your assigned advisor here.

MyAdvising.ucr.edu

The CNAS Undergraduate Academic Advising Center offers two convenient options for student advising appointments: in-person or virtual.  Advising appointments are the preferred method for discussing academic questions or concerns that may require more time.  You can see your advisor during an appointment to discuss topics like major changes, long-term academic plans, double-majors or minors, etc.  You can schedule your meeting with your assigned advisor at MyAdvising.ucr.edu.  Learn more about making advising appointments here.

 

General Undergraduate Advising Check-In's (GUAC)

The CNAS Undergraduate Academic Advising Center is proud to offer General Undergrad Advising Check-In's (GUAC) for students seeking quick and straightforward advising assistance.   Located in at 1223 Pierce Hall, our advisor are available throughout the day for drop-in advising sessions.  Learn more about seeing an advisor in person for GUAC here.

Email

Emailing is a convenient way for students to get in contact with their academic advisor. Please expect responses within 2-3 business days, and please note that this response time may extend during peak advising times. If you need detailed information for a more complex advising question, you should schedule an advising appointment with your assigned advisor. For quick and more general questions, you should take advantage of the General Undergrad Advising Check-In's (GUAC) option. Find more details about protocol and best practices when contacting your advisor here.
