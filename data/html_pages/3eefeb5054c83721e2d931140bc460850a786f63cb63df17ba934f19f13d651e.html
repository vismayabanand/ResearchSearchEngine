About englibnews
English (USA)

Share news about Stanford Libraries events, workshops, services, and tips with engineering students, faculty, and staff

To see the collection of prior postings to the list, visit the englibnews Archives. (The current archive is only available to the list members.)

Using englibnews
To post a message to all the list members, send email to englibnews@lists.stanford.edu.

You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription, in the sections below.

Subscribing to englibnews

Subscribe to englibnews by filling out the following form. You will be sent email requesting confirmation, to prevent others from gratuitously subscribing you. This is a private list, which means that the list of members is not available to non-members.
