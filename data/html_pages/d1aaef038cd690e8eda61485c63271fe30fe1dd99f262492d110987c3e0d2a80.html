NVIDIA Graduate Fellowships
– NVIDIA has long believed that investing in university talent is beneficial to the industry and key to our continued growth and success. The NVIDIA Graduate Fellowship Program now provides funding in the amount of up to $60,000 per award to Ph.D. students who are researching topics that will lead to major advances in accelerated computing and its applications.
