Service and Software Sunset Schedule

The policy for minimum software versions at Stanford is N-2, which is the current version plus the two most recently supported versions available. N-3 versions will be sunset four months later (rounded to the end of the month). At times, a particular service may have different needs and risks. In these instances, the service owner is empowered to establish a more stringent policy to ensure that the software used in their service is up-to-date and secure.

Below is a sunset schedule for prominent University IT services and other widely used software at Stanford.

Windows 10September 15, 2025Microsoft will no longer provide security updates for Windows 10 after October 14, 2025. MyDevices will begin marking Windows 10 devices as non-compliant on Sept. 15th, 2025 to ensure that these devices have been upgraded by the Microsoft deadline.
