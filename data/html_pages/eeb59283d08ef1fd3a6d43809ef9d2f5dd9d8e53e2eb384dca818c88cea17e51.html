TICKET INFORMATION

 

Tickets will be required for both students participating and their guests.

Students wishing to participate should begin by reviewing at minimum STEP 1 below so that they are aware of the process to become eligible to order tickets.  The ordering period will take place in Spring quarter.  Please continue to check back at this page for updates to ticketing information.  

We also ask that your email address in MyUCLA is kept up to date and that you pay attention to messages that may be coming from the UCLA engineering domain @seas.ucla.edu. This includes making sure your spam filters are updated so that commencement messages do not go to junk/spam.

 

IMPORTANT NOTICE: Students who elect to participate in commencement agree to remain for the full length of the ceremony out of respect for your fellow students and their families.

 

​Step 1 (REQUIRED)

Students need to convey their intent to graduate no later than Friday, April 18, 2025.

  • FALL 2024 graduates with degrees conferred are automatically eligible to participate and may proceed to Steps 2 – 5

  • WINTER 2025 graduates should automatically be eligible to participate and may proceed to Steps 2 – 5

  • SPRING 2025 and SUMMER 2025 are eligible to participate and will receive updates if they complete or have completed Steps 1 – 5

  • FALL 2025 students are not automatically eligible to participate in the engineering commencement.  Students who wish to  “walk/participate” this year should review the “SPECIAL REQUEST” information below and complete  Step 1 

UNDERGRADUATES

Engineering Undergraduates for Spring 2025*, Summer 2025 and Fall 2025 indicate they plan to graduate by completing the required online  degree audit process. Please make sure you have completed the degree audit process no later than Friday, April 18, 2025.   It is the degree audit process that flags an undergraduate for commencement and allows them to move on to the senior exit survey and commencement ticketing. *NOTE SPRING 2025 students should have completed the degree audit process last quarter, Winter 2025.

GRADUATES (MEng/MS/PHD)

If you are a degree candidate (regardless of whether you plan to participate in any commencement related activities) we ask that you begin by completing the Commencement Form for graduate students, no later than Friday, April 18, 2025, 11:59pm.

The Commencement Form is NOW CLOSED. Students are advised to check in with their Graduate Student Affairs Office. See contacts below.

Completing the form is the first step in identifying you as a degree candidate and determining your eligibility to participate in commencement, activate you for commencement ticketing (when it becomes available), and to be included in the commencement
printed program brochure.

Please select your respective Exit Survey.

We ask that you set aside 15-20 minutes to complete the survey in one sitting and that you do not exit or close out as this may result in an error.

Undergraduate Senior Exit Survey

PHD/MS/MEng Graduate Student Exit Survey

Step 3 (OPTIONAL) – Complete Online Name Card Registration 

 

Engineering students will have the option to complete an online name card registration form.* This will also allow us to display your name on the Pauley screen as it is announced while you cross the stage. The online form will ask how you would like your name displayed, as well as, the phonetic spelling of your name (your name the way it sounds). For help on your phonetic spelling, the form will have an example but you can also reference these guidelines.

Students who have FERPA RESTRICTIONS and/or ALL PUBLIC INFORMATION RESTIRCTIONS will NOT be sent an email with link to complete the Online Name Card Registration. A generic card will be available for you to complete the phonetic spelling and pronunciation of your name. Your name will NOT be displayed on the Pauley screen during the ceremony as it is announced while you cross the stage.

*Please note that you will be asked to provide your preferred email address on this link and, if you do so, you are consenting to receiving pictures and messages from GradImages, who are the photographers for our commencement ceremonies.

 

Check back soon for further details on when the online name card registration form will be sent for students to complete.

 

Submitting your name card is NOT a commencement ticket. Students MUST order their commencement tickets through MyUCLA (see Step 4 below).

​Step 4A – Order your student ticket and your guest tickets (via MyUCLA)

THE TICKET ORDERING PERIOD BEGINS FRIDAY, MAY 2, 2025 12:00 PM (NOON)

THE TICKET ORDERING PERIOD ENDS FRIDAY, MAY 23, 2025 11:59AM

Tickets are NOT first come, first served.   Students will have three weeks to complete their ticket orders. Login to MyUCLA (Beginning Friday, May 2 at 12 noon) > Select Academics > Select Commencement (in the Graduation category).

TICKET DETAILS: Eligible engineering students will be able to login to MyUCLA during the active ordering period and can order their student ticket and up to 10 guest tickets as follow:

  • (1) one student participation ticket (required)
  • (4) guest tickets total, where the first four (4) guest tickets are free (optional)
  • And (6) additional guest tickets at a cost of $20.00 per ticket (optional)

Students are allotted a maximum of 10 total guest tickets. All students and their guests are required to have a ticket to gain entrance into Pauley Pavilion and the student line up.

​Step 5 – Review Ticket Delivery and Billing Process

Commencement tickets are completely digital/mobile and accessible using Apple Wallet/Google Pay & Print at Home. The UCLA Central Ticket Office (CTO) will be sending digital electronic tickets via e-mail to your e-mail address (as it is indicated in MyUCLA).  The tickets will be sent from cto@tickets.ucla.edu. Please make sure your SPAM Filters do not block cto@tickets.ucla.edu  Distribution by e-mail from cto@tickets.ucla.edu will begin Tuesday, May 27, 2025 through June 13, 2025 and occur on a rolling basis as CTO begins to fulfill all orders.    You will receive e-mail messages from cto@tickets.ucla.edu that contain links for you to receive your tickets.  You will receive one e-mail message for your student participation ticket and one e-mail message for your guest tickets.

Be sure you check the email account you have on record in MyUCLA, and check that email account’s spam folder for your ticket messages.  Check your spam folder frequently, as you don’t want to miss receiving your tickets.  Some email providers empty spam folders regularly!

NOTE: If you are attending multiple ceremonies, for example you are a double major with the College of Letters and Science or you are PHD student also participating in the Graduate Division Hooding then note that you will receive separate student ticket e-mails and separate guest e-mails for each ceremony.  Therefore, you may receive a total of four email messages with links for you to claim your tickets for each ceremony. We advise you pay close attention to your e-mail notices and digital ticket information.

THE BILLING PROCESS Charges related to Commencement events will be posted to your Bruin Bill.  There will be two billing cycles where you may see charges posted.

  • First Billing Cycle:  Thursday, May 29, 2025
  • Second Billing Cycle:  Tuesday, June 17, 2025

Charges for tickets are assessed when the order is placed in MyUCLA .  The first four guest tickets are free; each additional guest ticket will be charged $20.00.  Once ordered, students are charged for guest tickets whether or not they are picked up or used.  NO REFUNDS ARE ISSUED.
