Calling all robotics enthusiasts!
 

From elementary to high school, our robotics faculty host events that introduce future engineers to grade-specific engineering concepts and kick off life-long dreams of a career in the industry.

ROBOTICS OUTREACH OPPORTUNITIES

“Both camps were just the right opportunity and time to expose, engage, motivate and challenge students coming out of the pandemic and inspire them to pursue a career in STEM.”

- Deepika Srivastava, STEAM and Innovation Coordinator at Redlands Unified School District

Read MorE ABOUT OUR ROBOTICS CAMPS 

FIRST LEGO LEAGUE FOR ELEMENTARY AND MIDDLE SCHOOL STUDENTS

As part of a larger national campaign, this annual fall event in Riverside brings together student teams to design their own solution to a scientific problem and build autonomous LEGO robots that perform a series of missions.

What we're looking for: student participants, community partners, student and community volunteers, and gifts.

SUMMER ROBOTICS CAMP FOR MIDDLE SCHOOL STUDENTS

In this summer robotics camp hosted in partnership with Redlands Unified School District, middle school students build functioning robots and use a controller application to interact with it - being introduced to college-level STEM activities in a low-stress, fun environment.

What we're looking for: community partners.
