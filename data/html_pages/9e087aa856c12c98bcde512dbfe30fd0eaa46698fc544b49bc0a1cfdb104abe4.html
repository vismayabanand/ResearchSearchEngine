Welcome to my webpage



I am Pamela Bhattacharya, and I was a graduate student at the Department of Computer Science , University of California Riverside (UCR). My advisor is Prof. Iulian Neamtiu and and I am a member of the RIPLE Lab. I successfully completed my PhD in Spring, 2012. I will be joining the Windows Reliability, Security and Privacy team from July, 2012 at Microsoft, Redmond.

My research interests are in developing tools that can improve software maintenance and facilitate empirical software engineering research at the same time, with focus on open source projects. I also have a background in compiler optimizations.

I was born and brought up in a town called Chinsurah in the state of West Bengal, India.
