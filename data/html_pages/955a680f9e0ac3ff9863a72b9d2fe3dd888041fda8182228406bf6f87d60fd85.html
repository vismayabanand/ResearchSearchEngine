2024 Commencement

PHOTOGRAPHY BY ELAN MIZRAHI

Congratulations to the School of Computer Science Class of 2024 on your graduation! As you celebrate this remarkable achievement, we are thrilled to reflect on the dedication and innovation you’ve demonstrated throughout your journey. Your hard work has prepared you to tackle the challenges of tomorrow, and we can’t wait to see all that you will accomplish in the years ahead. Remember, you are now part of a vibrant community of thinkers and creators — go out and make your mark on the world!
