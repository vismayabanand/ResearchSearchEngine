Our Mission

Our mission is to promote academic excellence, build strong communities, and develop professional mindsets among students of underrepresented minority groups, with the ultimate goal of developing leaders in the academy, industry, and society.

Our Mission
OME Vision

Imagine an MIT experience where all students are connected, happy, excelling, expanding their boundaries, and inspried to follow and achieve their passions.

OME Vision

OME works closely with students who are underrepresented — including African American, Native American, and Latino students — to ensure their academic success while building an essential community among undergraduates, faculty, and staff.
