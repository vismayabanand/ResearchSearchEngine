Academic Year & Term
Explore classes offered by term. Stanford operates on a 10-week, quarter-term academic calendar. This allows students to take up to 50 percent more classes during their time in college than possible on the semester system. Students are typically enrolled in classes for three terms – autumn, winter, spring – but Stanford also offers a summer term.
