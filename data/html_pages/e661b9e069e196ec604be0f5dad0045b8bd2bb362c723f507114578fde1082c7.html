K&L Gates Presidential Fellowships, are supported by the K&L Gates Endowment for Ethics and Computational Technologies at Carnegie Mellon University. As the ethical and policy issues surrounding artificial intelligence and other computing technologies take center stage, global law firm K&L Gates LLP provided a gift to help ensure Carnegie Mellon University's leadership in this emerging field. The K&L Gates Presidential Fellowships support doctoral students whose studies relate to the field of ethics and computational science and technology.
