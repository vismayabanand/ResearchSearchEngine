Graduation Gear at the Stanford Bookstore

Main content start

Academic attire is required to participate in many Commencement Weekend activities and is available to rent or purchase from the Stanford Bookstore. The vast majority rent regalia, and those who regularly participate in Commencement exercises, such as faculty, may opt to purchase.

Important Dates and Deadlines

Monday, June 2 – Saturday, June 14: Student rental regalia available for order and pick up

Sunday, June 15: Rental regalia returns at the Stanford Bookstore

Regalia Rentals

All graduates and faculty are required to wear a cap and gown (regalia) to process in the main Commencement ceremony and Baccalaureate celebration. 

Student regalia is available for rent in person at the Stanford Bookstore from June 2–14. See rental ala cart options. 

Faculty regalia will be available for pick up at the Stanford Bookstore June 9th - 13th. 

Faculty who missed the deadline to pre-order can still get generic rental regalia during open stock at the bookstore, June 9th - 13th. Please note that the hoods provided for walk-up faculty rentals will have Stanford Colors in the lining, and not all degree colors will be available.

Regalia Purchase Options

The Stanford Bookstore is the only authorized and licensed seller of Stanford regalia. Custom regalia is exclusively for purchase. In partnership with Jostens, the bookstore offers standard and custom Stanford regalia. Orders for this year's Commencement are now closed.

Learn more about custom regalia

Rental Regalia Returns

Student returns:

Gowns can be returned after the ceremony to either the Stanford Bookstore (in front of the store) or to the Stanford Bookstore popup locations throughout the Stadium during the Sunday Commencement ceremony. Gowns, hoods, and PhD tams may be kept throughout the weekend’s festivities and should be returned by Monday. Mortarboards and tassels are yours to keep.

Faculty returns:

Gowns can be returned after the ceremony to either the Stanford Bookstore (in front of the store) or to the Stanford Bookstore popup locations throughout the Stadium during the Sunday Commencement ceremony. Regalia may be kept throughout the weekend’s festivities and should be returned by Monday.
