Transforming Southern California and the World

The UCLA Henry Samueli School of Engineering and Applied Science includes more than 6,500 undergraduate and graduate students and 190 full-time faculty members. Established in 1945, UCLA Samueli is known as the birthplace of the internet, and where countless other fields took some of their first steps â€” from artificial intelligence to reverse osmosis, from mobile communications to human prosthetics.
