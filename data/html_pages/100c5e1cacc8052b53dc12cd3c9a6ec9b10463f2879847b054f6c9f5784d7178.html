Please keep note of the following when scheduling an appointment:

  • All appointments are being held virtually (via Microsoft Teams) and in Pacific Time (US & Canada)
  • Do NOT schedule more than one appointment at a time. Any additional appointments will be cancelled.
  • To cancel an appointment, please see the Appointment Cancellation Instructions.
