We are a group of EECS graduate students trained as peer mediators to support the graduate community and serve as a first point of contact in dealing with stress, however big or small. We are here to listen to concerns in a non-intimidating and confidential environment. We can mediate disputes when appropriate. If you are in a difficult situation or just want somebody to talk to, please come visit one of us. We can help you to better understand and more effectively manage your situation, and/or direct you to other resources for further assistance.

You can think of us as your confidential best friends.

To reach all of us at once, send an email to eecs-refs [at] mit [dot] edu.
