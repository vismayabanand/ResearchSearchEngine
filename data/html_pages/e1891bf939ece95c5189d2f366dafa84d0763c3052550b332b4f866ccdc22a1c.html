Introducing Software at CMU

We understand the importance of having the right software to get your work done efficiently. We are pleased to announce the launch of our new Software at CMU site in partnership with the University Contracts Office (UCO), Procurement, the Eberly Center, the University Bookstore, and Computing Services.

What is Software at CMU?

This is your one-stop destination for accessing a wide range of software titles. Whether you’re faculty, staff, or a student, you'll find the tools you need to succeed.

Why Use Software at CMU?

  • Convenience: Easily find and access the software you need.
  • Cost-Effective: Many software licenses are available at no cost to eligible customers.
  • Streamlined Process: Follow our easy request software process for other options.

How Does It Work?

  1. Browse: Explore the extensive list of available software.
  2. Check Eligibility: Verify if you meet the eligibility requirements.
  3. Access: Download and start using the software right away.
Visit Software at CMU to find the titles you need to succeed in your academic and professional endeavors.
