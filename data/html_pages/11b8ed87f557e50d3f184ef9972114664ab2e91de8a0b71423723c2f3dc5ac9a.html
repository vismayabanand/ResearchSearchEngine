Verify Eligibility for Employment (I-9)

Main content start

To comply with U.S. law, Stanford must verify eligibility for employment for all new employees – within three business days of hire – by collecting a Form I-9 and reviewing original documents that establish the employee's eligibility to work in the United States.

Make an I-9 Appointment

  • You are responsible for scheduling an in-person I-9 appointment to meet a University Human Resources (UHR) representative to review and confirm your ability to work in the United States. Stanford makes it easy, with multiple locations on varying days of the week.
  • Some departments and schools such as SLAC do not utilize the Central I-9 appointment locations to complete the verification process. Please check with your local HR point of contact to see if an appointment is needed. 
  • At each location, there should be a sign (or information in your confirmation email) that instructs you to call a phone number when you arrive, as the buildings may require entry assistance.

Documentation to Bring to the Appointment

Employees must present original, current, and unexpired physical documents to verify employment eligibility. Photocopies and electronic copies are not acceptable. Expired IDs are not acceptable. Acceptable documents are listed on page 2 of the federal government's Form I-9.

New and rehired employees who have not completed the Form I-9

  1. Download the Form I-9 and complete Section 1 no later than the first day of employment
  2. Schedule an appointment at a location using the buttons below (appointment must be within three business days from the first day employment)
  3. Print and bring the Form I-9 with the acceptable document(s) to the appointment

Current employees that need to re-verify due to a change in status, employment authorization, or employment authorization documents

  1. Schedule an appointment at a UHR location using the buttons below
  2. Bring current and valid document(s) to the appointment
  3. If possible, please bring recently expired documents to the appointment to ensure we have all necessary documents on file. If you have held prior jobs at Stanford, please bring the original I-9 documentation that was provided for your original start date(s) in addition to the current and valid documents

Schedule an Appointment

  1. Select a convenient location below to see all the dates and times available
  2. Select your date and time (15-minute time slot)
  3. Enter your information to complete the request
  4. A confirmation email will be sent with additional instructions
