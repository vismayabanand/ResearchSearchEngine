Shaping the Digital Future

Welcome to the Societal Computing Program at Carnegie Mellon University, where we tackle the complex challenges involving the impact of computing on society. With a multi-disciplinary approach, our PhD program prepares tomorrow's leaders to design technologies addressing societal needs and guide their implementation. Explore our cutting-edge research, innovative curriculum, and join us in shaping the digital landscape for a better tomorrow.

Our Program

Our PhD program in Societal Computing, offered through Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science, provides the techniques, theories, and research methods to address societal issues and create technologies that impact society.
