Student Resources

New to SCS? Welcome! This page is a collection of links, resources and information relevant to students in the School of Computer Science. In addition, you will find resources relevant to the greater university. Some of the systems and resources referenced on this page may not be provided or managed by SCS Computing Facilities (SCSCF), and are included for your convenience.

If you require support, you can visit our Help Desk, available Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm with extended 24x7 coverage for critical issues and SCS machine room-hosted equipment. You can reach us via phone at 412-268-4231, you can submit a ticket or email help@cs.cmu.edu.
