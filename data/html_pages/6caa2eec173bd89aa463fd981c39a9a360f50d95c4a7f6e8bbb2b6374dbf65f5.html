Welcome to CTL Student Learning Programs

As part of the Stanford Center for Teaching and Learning (CTL), our programs are designed to teach transferable academic skills, create a safe space to take intellectual risks, and help all undergraduate and graduate students become self-directed learners. We hope to provide you with the tools necessary to help you achieve your academic goals—no matter where you are in your learning process.
