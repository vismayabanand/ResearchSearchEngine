Qualcomm Innovation Fellowships
– (QIF) recognize that research and development is the key to harnessing the power of imagination and to discovering new possibilities. They have established a fellowship that promotes Qualcomm's core values of innovation, execution and teamwork. The goal is to enable students to pursue their futuristic innovative ideas.
