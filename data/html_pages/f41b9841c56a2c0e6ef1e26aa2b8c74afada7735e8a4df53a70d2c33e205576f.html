Postdoc position in MetaboloGenomics

Dr. Mohimani’s MetaboloGenomics lab at the Ray and Stephanie Lane Computational Biology Department is looking for a Post-Doc who will cultivate thousands of microbial isolates and extract their DNA and metabolome for next generation sequencing and tandem mass spectrometry data collection. This is a joint project with Pelletier lab at Oak Ridge National Laboratory. The Post-Doc will spend a portion of their time collected data at ORNL (Oak Ridge, TN), and another part analyzing the data at Carnegie Mellon University (Pittsburgh, PA).

Qualifications:

  • Doctorate degree in Computer Science or Bioinformatics
  • 1-3 years of relevant Reporting and Analytics experience
  • Excellent analytical and oral/written communication skills

Application may be found at https://apply.interfolio.com/93270
