Computer Science and AI Training From the Best

The Executive and Professional Education program at Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science connects organizations and individuals to our computer scientists and educators who are pioneers in their respective fields. 

We accomplish this through a combination of custom courses, online courses and certificate programs. Our custom courses are personalized to meet the upskilling needs of the enterprise with one or more of our academic courses serving as the technical underpinning. Our online courses and certificate programs are open enrollment and are suited for individuals desiring to upskill in a specific area. We also offer dedicated cohorts of online courses and certificate programs for organizations wanting to upskill 30 or more software engineers at a time.

Custom Courses

Our custom courses are built upon the foundation of one or more of our academic courses providing a robust technical framework. Designed and delivered by our esteemed research and teaching faculty, these courses are adapted to match the desired level of rigor, spanning from undergraduate to master’s or PhD levels, aligning with your specific learning objectives. With a limited cohort size of up to 30 students, we guarantee an exceptionally interactive learning experience. * Classroom simulated by DALL.E.

Online Courses and Certificate Programs

Our online courses and certificate programs stand out as some of our most coveted offerings on campus. Serving as a cost-effective alternative to our custom courses, they offer a convenient pathway to stay updated on the latest advancements in computer science and artificial intelligence. Immerse yourself in our diverse portfolio of online courses and certificate programs, meticulously designed by our faculty to equip you with a competitive edge
