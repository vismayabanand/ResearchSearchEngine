This page is designed to help you navigate the university’s forms, petitions, and request processes.

Important: The processing times listed below apply to final approval & processing by the Registrar's Office only. If your form requires approval from another office (e.g., your academic department), the total time required to process it after submission may be increased.

Waiting on the approval of a submitted form?

After you submit an eForm, it will go through an approval workflow that may include multiple waypoints. You may check the status of your pending form at any time in the eForms portal. 

Note: the listed deadlines relate to form submission. All forms submitted by the deadline are considered "on time," regardless of approval status.

Processing: 1-2 weeks after the form is received by the Registrar's Office

Open: Only available during the open enrollment period for the effective quarter.

More info:

  • Use the form below to apply for an auditor status (for those affiliated with Stanford only; no fee) in order to audit courses. Consult the Bulletin for applicable policies. See also Cardinal at Work.
  • Obtain the approval of the instructor and department administrator via email, and upload a PDF of each email approval when you submit your request.

Where: Submit application for Auditor Status online (SUNet ID login required)

Processing: 3-5 business days

More info: If you are an admitted, non-matriculated student, obtain the approval(s) of the instructor(s) of the courses and submit the completed and signed form to the email address at the top of the PDF.

Where: Downloadable PDF: Course Enrollment Request - Nonmatric GR, not available in eForms Portal

Processing: 3-5 business days

Deadlines:

  • Late drop and change of grading basis requests are only considered for the current quarter, and must be submitted by the grading deadline of the effective quarter.
  • Late adds, section changes, changes to variable units, withdrawals, and extensions of "Incomplete" ("I") grades are only considered within one year of when you took the course. (For instance, if during Spring 24-25 a student wants to make a retroactive change to their enrollment, they will only be able to request changes as far back as Spring 23-24.)

Where: Axess eForms portal

Processing: One week

Note: For enrollment petitions, coterm students should use the petition protocol applicable to the course: Use the undergraduate process (via Academic Advising) if the course is on the undergraduate transcript; use the graduate process if the course is on the graduate transcript.

Deadline: Term withdrawal deadline of the effective quarter

More info: Use to petition for reinstatement and enrollment if you were discontinued this quarter due to no enrollment. Students who were discontinued in a previous term must instead submit the Reinstatement in Graduate Study Application.

Where: Axess eForms portal

Processing: 3-5 business days

Deadline: Deadline depends on type of exception requested

More info: Use to request second repeat, late add, late section change, late change of unit total, late study list, or late withdraw

Where: PDF available from the Academic Advising Requests for Exceptions to Academic Policy page

Deadline: Final Study List Deadline of effective quarter

Open: First day of the effective quarter

Where: PDF available from the Academic Advising Requests for Exceptions to Academic Policy page

Deadline: Final Study List Deadline of effective quarter

Open: First day of the effective quarter

More info: Academic Advising Requests for Exceptions to Academic Policy and Can I Take More Than 20 Units in a Quarter?

Where: Axess eForms portal

Deadline: Second repeat petitions need to be submitted by the Final Study List Deadline of effective quarter

Open: eForm is open but submissions should adhere to above deadlines

More info: Second repeats (third enrollments in non-repeatable courses) will still adhere to the Repeat Policy.

Where: Axess eForms portal

Processing: 2 weeks

Deadline: Submissions are only considered within one year of when the student took the course. (For instance, if during Spring 24-25 a student wants to make a retroactive change to their enrollment, they will only be able to request changes as far back as Spring 23-24.)

 

Deadline: Drops and unit adjustments by the Final Study List Deadline; withdrawals by the Withdrawal and Grading Basis Deadline

Open: When enrollment opens for the effective quarter

Students must discuss this change in enrollment with an AARC advisor to determine the impact on athletic eligibility prior to submitting this form.  This form will only be processed following approval from the Compliance Services Office in the Department of Athletics, Physical Education and Recreation.

Where: PDF in Axess eForms portal

Processing: 3-5 business days

Deadline: Due prior to the Final Study List Deadline or Grading Basis Deadline for your particular alternate-schedule Summer class. May not be used after the applicable deadline

More info: Prior to the Preliminary Study List Deadline, any student wishing to remove their final class from their study list may submit this form. After the Preliminary Study List Deadline, this form should only be used by students enrolled in non-standard scheduled Summer Classes who are unable to update their study lists in Axess after the Final Study List

Where: Axess eForms portal

Processing: 3-5 business days

Where: Downloadable PDF: Change of Advisor or Reading Committee Memeber (submit this form to your department).

Leave, Withdraw, and Return/Reinstate

Deadline: Final Study List Deadline of the effective quarter

Open: Two weeks before Axess opens for enrollment for the effective quarter.

​More info: Students currently on an approved Leave of Absence (LOA) should use the eForms listed below to request to return for classes earlier than stated in their original LOA submission.

Processing: 1-2 weeks

Where: eForm in Axess eForms portal

  • Undergraduate Early Return from Leave of Absence
  • Graduate Early Return from Leave of Absence
  • Coterminal Early Return from Leave of Absence (only when both UG and GR careers are still active)

Deadline: Term Withdrawal Deadline of effective quarter

Open: When enrollment opens for the effective quarter

More info: Registrar's Leave of Absence website

Where: Axess eForms portal

Processing: 1-2 weeks

Deadline: Term withdrawal deadline of the effective quarter

More info: Use to petition for reinstatement and Leave of Absence if you were discontinued this quarter due to no enrollment. Students who were discontinued in a previous term must instead follow the applicable reinstatement process (Reinstatement in Graduate Study Application and/or Request to Return and Register for Undergraduate Students)

Where: eForm via https://eforms.stanford.edu/ (note: not the standard path to the Axess eForms portal)

Processing: 3-5 business days

Deadline: Term withdrawal deadline, as stated in the Academic Calendar. 

More info:

  • Graduate students: GAP Program Discontinuation and Reinstatement

Where: Axess eForms portal

Processing: 2-3 weeks for an immediate request or 2-3 weeks after the grades due deadline for a end of term request.

Deadline: Preliminary Study List Deadline of the quarter of requested re-enrollment

More info: If you are an admitted graduate student who has not maintained continuous registration (and has not been on an approved leave of absence), you must apply for reinstatement. After completing this form, submit it to your department for approval and they will submit it to Enrollment & Records. If approved for reinstatement, you will be billed for the reinstatement fee. Information for Coterms

Where: Downloadable PDF: Reinstatement in Graduate Study Application

Submit via email to: enrollment@stanford.edu

Processing: 1-2 weeks

Deadline: Preliminary Study List Deadline of the quarter of requested re-enrollment

Where: Downloadable PDF: Coterm Request for Reinstatement Application

Forms signed by the graduate department should be submitted to your Undergraduate Academic Advisor

Processing: 1-2 weeks

If you are an admitted coterminal student who has not conferred your undergraduate degree and have not maintained continuous registration (and has not been on an approved leave of absence), you must apply for reinstatement. After completing this form, submit it to your graduate department for their determination. Once your graduate department has signed the form, students are required to meet with their Undergraduate Academic Advisor to review and submit the PDF form. If approved for reinstatement into graduate study (as determined by the graduate department), you will be billed for the reinstatement fee. Information for Coterms.

For more information, please see the Academic Advising Requests for Exceptions to Academic Policy page, as well as the Returning to Stanford page.

Undergraduate petitions related to academic standing and policies are administered by the Office of the Vice Provost for Undergraduate Education (VPUE). Please see the Academic Advising Requests for Exceptions to Academic Policy page, as well as the Returning to Stanford page.
