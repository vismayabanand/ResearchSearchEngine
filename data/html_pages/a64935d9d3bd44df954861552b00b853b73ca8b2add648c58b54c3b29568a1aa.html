Get the Software You Need

Search for licensed software available for use or purchase by the Stanford community

The Software at Stanford site provides information about software available for use or purchase on campus through several providers such as University IT, Stanford University Libraries, Stanford SmartMart, and the Stanford Bookstore.

If you are a campus provider of software for general use and would like your products to be added to this site, please contact the staff of the University IT Software Licensing office.
