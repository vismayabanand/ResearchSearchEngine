Business, education, and engineering grad programs ranked high
UC Riverside’s business, education, and engineering programs placed high in U.S. News &amp; World Report’s 2025 rankings of graduate programs in the U.S.&nbsp; The School of Business climbed 17 positions for its part-time Master of Business Administration, or MBA, program to rank No. 56 in the nation. The Marlan and Rosemary Bourns College of Engineering, or BCOE, ranked No. 89 overall, saw increases in five programs. The School of Education ranked No. 90 overall among 258 schools with doctoral-level programs.
