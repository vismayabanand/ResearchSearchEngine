EarthWorks

    Geospatial datasets

    Data that includes location information (coordinates) and is natively usable in GIS software and services such as ArcGIS, QGIS, WFS, and WMS. Geospatial data types include vector data, such as Shapefile, GeoJSON, and GeoPackage, and raster data, such as GeoTIFFs, grid files, and georeferenced maps. There are 24,078 datasets in EarthWorks.

    Maps

    Images of paper maps that have been scanned and/or georeferenced. These digital cartographic resources cover all parts of the world and include topographical maps, historical maps, nautical charts, thematic maps, and atlases. There are 35,151 maps in EarthWorks.
