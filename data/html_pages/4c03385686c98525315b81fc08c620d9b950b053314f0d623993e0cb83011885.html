    Courses

    Summer Courses

    6-10 week courses that represent UCLA's academic breadth, offered fully in person or hybrid (in person/online). Earn credit in a subject perfect for your path.
    Summer Online

    Courses that are designed for online learning. Earn credit from wherever you are in the world.
    Summer Intensives

    Three-week courses offered in an accelerated format. Earn the same amount of credit in less time.

    Programs

    College/Professional Summer Institutes

    Innovative UCLA-credit programs with a career focus and professional development opportunities in business, architecture, film, and more.
    Summer College Immersion Program

    The UCLA undergraduate experience for high school students. Earn college credit and gain insight into college life.
    Precollege Summer Institutes

    Immersive and intensive UCLA-credit programs for advanced high school students in STEM, social science, creative arts and more.
    Public Leadership Impact Program

    Begin your journey in addressing issues that define our world. Enhance problem-solving capabilities and learn how strategic decisions drive real-world change.

Student Type Deadline

Starting June 21, payment is due immediately for all sessions to avoid drops for non-payment or financial holds. If there is an outstanding BruinBill balance, it may result in all courses being drops and/or financial holds.

UCLA and other UC financial aid students may be eligible for a payment extension in the event aid does not disburse by the payment deadline.

Grading Basis Change Deadline:

Courses with an optional grading basis allow you to choose either a Letter grade, Passed/Not Passed grade (for students without a Bachelor’s degree), or Satisfactory/Not Satisfactory grade (for students with a Bachelor’s degree).

Before changing your grade type to anything other than a letter grade, please consult with an academic advisor at your home institution to verify whether you need a specific grade type to apply the course toward degree requirements for your progress in your degree program.

Payment Deadline:

June 1, 2025 

All deadlines are at 5:00PM Pacific Time (PST) unless otherwise indicated.

Please submit full payment on MyUCLA under the “Finances and Jobs” tab and then “BruinBill” no later than 5pm on June 1. Make sure to account for payment processing time in advance.

Note: Housing fees for residential students and RES PRC 97XD fees for SCIP+ students will be added on May 16. Any charges added to the BruinBill after the payment deadline are due immediately to avoid being dropped.
