ARCS: Achievement Rewards for College Scientists
ARCS recognizes outstanding students who have a record of past achievement and who show exceptional promise of making a significant contribution to the scientific and technological strength of the nation. Learn More 		Doctoral Students by Nomination Winter quarter
CCSRE: Center for Comparative Studies in Race and Ethnicity Emerging Scholars Graduate Fellowship
CCSRE provides three-year fellowships to incoming doctoral students, who are interested in the study of the meanings, processes, and consequences of race, ethnicity and inequality. Learn More 		Incoming Doctoral Students by Nomination Winter quarter
DARE: Diversifying Academia, Recruiting Excellence
The DARE Doctoral Fellowship prepares students from a wide range of backgrounds to pursue and succeed in academic careers. Learn More 		Doctoral Students by Application Winter quarter
EDGE Doctoral Fellowship Program
EDGE aims to recruit and retain outstanding doctoral students who will contribute to the richness of their academic fields and degree programs. Learn More 		Incoming Doctoral Students by Nomination Winter Quarter
Gerald J. Lieberman Fellowship
The Lieberman Fellowship recognizes doctoral students who have demonstrated broad potential for leadership in academia. Learn More 		Doctoral Students by Nomination Winter quarter
RAISE: Research, Action, and Impact through Strategic Engagement Doctoral Fellowship
The RAISE Doctoral Fellowship Program supports doctoral students who are motivated to make positive contributions to their communities and the world. Learn More 		Doctoral Students by Application Winter quarter
SGF: Stanford Graduate Fellowship in Science & Engineering
SGF supports outstanding incoming and continuing doctoral students in science and engineering. Learn More 		Incoming Doctoral Students, Doctoral Students by Nomination Winter quarter
Shoucheng Zhang Graduate Fellowship
The Shoucheng Zhang Graduate Fellowship supports outstanding doctoral students who are pursuing innovative, interdisciplinary research in the broad field of quantum science and engineering. Learn More 		Incoming Doctoral Students by Nomination Winter quarter
SIGF: Stanford Interdisciplinary Graduate Fellowship
SIGF supports doctoral students whose research crosses traditional disciplinary boundaries. Learn More 		Doctoral Students by Application Winter quarter
