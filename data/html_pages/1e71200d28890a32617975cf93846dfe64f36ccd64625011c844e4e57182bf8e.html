Career Opportunities in S3D

Faculty Positions

The Software and Societal Systems Department seeks candidates with outstanding academic credentials and compelling research vision for teaching-track and tenure-track faculty appointments. We enthusiastically encourage applicants across all areas of software engineering foundations and applications to apply.

Staff Positions

The Software and Societal Systems Department and Carnegie Mellon University offer excellent opportunities for careers in higher education. All staff job postings are centralized through the university’s human resources web site, Careers @ Carnegie Mellon. Please note that due to the variety and volume of opportunities, all candidates must work through the central university site.
