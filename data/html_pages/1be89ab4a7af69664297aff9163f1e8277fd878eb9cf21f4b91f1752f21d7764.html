There is a lot more to Campus Housing than just…Housing.

Where you live during your time at UCR will actually impact your engagement, access to faculty and resources, GPA, and time to degree.   

But going to college is about even more than classes, exams, and getting a degree. It is a place to find your “community,” make personal choices, and make your mark. 

Get the most out of your college experience by living on campus!

Our four Residence Halls are open to First-Year students, Transfer students, as well as returning residents.

Contracts are now available!

Seven Campus Apartment Communities welcome Continuing, Transfer, and Graduate students. 

Apply now!

Family Housing is available to students at all class levels who are also supporting families of their own. 

Apply early!

Annual Security and Fire Safety Report

The Jeanne CLERY Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act of 1998, commonly referred to as the "CLERY Act," requires institutions of higher education receiving federal financial aid to report specified crime statistics on college campuses and to provide other safety and crime information to members of the campus community. This report is available on the UC Riverside Chief Compliance website.
