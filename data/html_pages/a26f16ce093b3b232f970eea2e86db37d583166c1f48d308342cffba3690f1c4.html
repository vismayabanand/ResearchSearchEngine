Judith A. Resnik Award, Carnegie Mellon University
--The Judith A. Resnik Award was established in 1990 to recognize and encourage an outstanding woman senior in the sciences or engineering planning to attend graduate school. The awardee receives a monetary award of $500, a certificate of recognition and their name is added to the Judith A. Resnik Award Recipient plaque.

  • Stephanie Rosenthal, 2007 (Honorable Mention)

  • Elena Balestreire, 2001
