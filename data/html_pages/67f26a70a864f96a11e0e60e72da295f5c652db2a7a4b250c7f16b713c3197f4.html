Visiting Scholars: Maximizing Research's Impact

Theres synergy between CS research and our visiting scholars from Hitachi: Chi Heem Wong and Gaku Morio. A recent interview with our esteemed guests highlights the energy and passion that emerges from our visiting scholar program.

Chi Heem is a Hitachi Senior Research Scientist applying machine learning and data science techniques to solve problems in finance, supply chain, and the heavy industry. He has worked with Percy Liang and Jure Leskovec. Gaku is a Hitachi Researcher of NLP and AI, pursuing R&D of cutting-edge technologies regarding the intersection of finance and climate change. He has worked with Chris Manning.

The students and professors I work with are smart, hardworking, and committed to the highest standard of research. - Chi Heem

I think Stanford is a stimulating yet friendly and open environment. The professors are open to discussing various research ideas, and they provide generous help with everything from experimental design to writing papers. - Gaku
