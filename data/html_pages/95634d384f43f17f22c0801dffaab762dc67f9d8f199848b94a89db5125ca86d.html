Save the Date: Family Weekend will be Feb. 21-22

November 14, 2024 2:53 pm
Family Weekend is an annual event held to provide families of current undergraduates a glimpse into their student’s life and experience at Stanford University. Registration will be required this year and will open in December. More information is available on the Family Weekend website.

New websites support freedom of expression and Title VI process

September 25, 2024 3:35 pm
In a recent message to campus, Stanford Provost Jenny Martinez and Vice Provost for Student Affairs Michele Rasmussen shared two new resources regarding freedom of expression and the new Title VI process.

Resources to support your student's mental health and well-being at Stanford

September 14, 2024 2:54 pm
The Mental Health Resources at Stanford website has a comprehensive list of the many campus mental health and well-being resources for students, including ones centered on identity and community. Students helped reimagine this website with staff and created six themes for categorizing the types of resources to make it easier for students to access help.
