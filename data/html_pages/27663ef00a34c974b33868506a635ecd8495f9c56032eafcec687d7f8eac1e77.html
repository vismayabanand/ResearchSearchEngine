About UC Riverside's Computer Engineering Program

Breadcrumb
  •  
  • About UC Riverside's Computer Engineering Program

Computing is at the heart of the digital information revolution that is reshaping our world. At the University of California, Riverside's Computer Engineering Program at the Marlan and Rosemary Bourns College of Engineering, we are at the forefront of the science and technology behind these revolutionary hardware and software systems. Computing is in every aspect of our lives - from the computers that we interact with directly in our laptops, desktops and smart phones, those in data centers making up the cloud, to Internet of Things that brings it all together. 

As we aim to create computing solutions for a modern world, our Computer Engineering program rivals that of top departments in the country and prepares students at all levels in this fast-moving and growing discipline. We offer undergraduate degrees, master's degrees and an accelerated master's program - all of which combine offerings from both our Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) departments.
