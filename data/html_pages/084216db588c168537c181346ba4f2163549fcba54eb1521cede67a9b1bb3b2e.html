About recruiting
English (USA)

The Computer Forum provides students with a unique opportunity to meet potential employers in a relaxed and focused environment through on-campus interviews, information sessions, career strategy workshops, company tours, and Career Fair. For additional information, please visit
https://forum.stanford.edu/careers/recruiting-benefits/student-recruiting-policies.

The recruiting@lists.stanford.edu mailing list is the main way the Computer Forum keeps students up-to-date regarding upcoming visits by Forum companies, job announcements, and other recruiting activities.

New recruiting platform: 12twenty To ensure your attendance at our upcoming events, please take a moment to sign up using the provided Google Form link https://forms.gle/dhcLegb2WF5qPqB28

Note that ONLY the Forum staff can email to this list and we ONLY make announcements for our member companies (with minor exceptions), so you won't get peppered with junk mail!

You can also find us on Slack stanford-wlwj.slack.com and follow the CS Department LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/school/stanford-university-department-of-computer-science/ and Computer Forum: https://www.linkedin.com/company/stanford-computer-forum/

If interested in non-affiliate job postings, please join cs-careers@lists.stanford.edu



To see the collection of prior postings to the list, visit the recruiting Archives. (The current archive is only available to the list members.)

Using recruiting
To post a message to all the list members, send email to recruiting@lists.stanford.edu.

You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription, in the sections below.
