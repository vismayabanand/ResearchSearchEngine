Partnerships

Carnegie Mellon’s School of Computer Science is a world leader in innovative thinking. Solving real-world problems is what we do best.

We offer a variety of partnership opportunities. In addition to research, recruitment and workforce development, there are many exciting educational projects and programs, capstone projects, and outreach programs.

Contact us to get started

The Partnerships Team is always available to help you connect with the SCS community. We also publish a newsletter, Engage, that highlights ways to partner with students, staff and faculty. You can read past issues here. We look forward to hearing from you soon!
