Computer Science Department Ph.D. Scholarship
–Established by an anonymous alumnus, this scholarship was created to help other Computer Science Department Ph.D students at Carnegie Mellon who are doing innovative research with the potential to change the field in which they are studying. The hope of our alumnus is that when the recipients of this fellowship graduate and are successful, they will inturn endow their own fellowships to help the next generation of students achieve their research goals.
