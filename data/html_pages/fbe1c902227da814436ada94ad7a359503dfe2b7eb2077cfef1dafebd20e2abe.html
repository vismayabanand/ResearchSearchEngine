October 17 | 8 a.m. - 4 p.m.

Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center, Stanford University

Mosey on down to the most thrilling shindig this side of the digital divide, where the dust will fly and the bytes will buzz as we explore the ins and outs of cybersecurity and privacy like never before. Through riveting discussions and student-focused AI innovations, the 2024 Cybersecurity and Privacy Festival aims to unravel the complexities of safeguarding privacy and security in an AI-driven landscape.

Whether you're a seasoned cyber sheriff or looking to learn the ropes — there's something for everyone.

All Stanford University, Stanford Health Care, SLAC, and the broader higher education communities are invited.

Live Recording of CISO Series Podcast

​

1:30 - 2:30 p.m. | McCaw Hall, Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center 

David Spark and his CISO guests make their first university appearance to record an episode of their popular show, CISO Series Podcast. No matter where you are in your cybersecurity journey, this show is accessible to both current and aspiring cybersecurity professionals. We'll pack our show with insightful discussion about security leadership, and dealing with cyber threats. Plus, we'll play our popular games "What's Worse?!", "What Is Dave's Mom Talking About?" and answer your questions. Guaranteed fun!

Host: David Spark

Founder and Executive Producer,
CISO Series

Guest: Amy Steagall

Chief Information Security Officer,
Stanford University IT

Guest: Michael Tran Duff

Chief Information Security and Data Privacy Officer,
Harvard University IT

Showdown at the AI Corral Pitch Competition

3 - 4 p.m. | McCaw Hall, Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center 

Gather 'round for an unforgettable showdown where Stanford students will have the opportunity to present their security and AI focused ideas to a panel of industry experts, investors, and potential partners.

It's gonna be a showdown for the ages!

Lockpicking Session

9 a.m. - 4 p.m |  McCaw Hall, Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center

Lockpicking tables will be available in McCaw Hall during the day, allowing you to lockpick while enjoying the speaker sessions.
