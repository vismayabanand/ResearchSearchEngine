UC Riverside is a tapestry of trailblazers — from first-generation students to globally recognized researchers — working together to accelerate bold solutions and transform lives.

Ranked No. 1 in the nation for social mobility and in the top 1.3% of universities worldwide, UCR has created a new model for what a great public research university can help you achieve!

Important Dates and Deadlines

First Years & Transfers

May 10

Housing deadline to receive a guaranteed space and get priority access to the room self-selection process.

Transfers

June 1

Deadline for admitted students to say “YES” to UCR. Submit the Statement of Intent to Register (SIR).

First Years & Transfers

Aug. 1

Deadline for admitted students to complete their Statement of Legal Residency (SLR), to verify if they are a resident of the state of California for tuition purposes.
