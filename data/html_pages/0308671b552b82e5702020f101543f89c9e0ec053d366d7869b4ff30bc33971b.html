This 15-videotape set of videotapes enables you to experience the presentations at the 1997 Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing Conference. Inspired by the legacy of Admiral Grace Murray Hopper, a pioneer in the field of computing and an inspiration to women and scientists everywhere, the conference encourages women by making them aware of the possibilities and rewards available to women in computing.
