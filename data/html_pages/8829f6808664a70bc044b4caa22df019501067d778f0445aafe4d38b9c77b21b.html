Flu Shots

Free for UCSHIP students, $25 for Non-SHIP students

POP-UP FLU VACCINATION FAIRS: The Ashe Center hosts pop-up influenza vaccination (flu shot) fairs in Bruin Plaza during flu season. When those events are scheduled, we will list them here.

Flu shots are $0 for UCSHIP students and $25 for waiver students. Fees are billed directly to the Bruin Bill, and students must present a valid Bruin Card to participate. No appointment necessary.

During flu season, UCLA students have options for receiving their annual Influenza vaccine (flu shot):

  • UCSHIP Students: Flu shots do not require a referral if administered at a local pharmacy.
  • Students can attend a flu vaccination pop-up event (various pop-ups scheduled on campus throughout flu season, see Events Calendar for details on upcoming events)
  • Students can ask their PCP for a flu shot during a routine visit
  • Students can schedule a flu shot using the Patient Portal
