About

The PolyArch research group, lead by Tony Nowatzki, explores a broad range of ideas in architecture specialization. Our work is broadly about breaking and reforming traditional abstractions across applications, programming languages, compilers, the hardware/software interface, and microarchitectures, with the goal of enabling high productivity and performance with increasingly parallel and heterogeneous architectures. We develop novel ISAs, accelerator architectures and their compilers, accelerator design-automation techniques, and develop open-source architecture design frameworks.
