Welcome to FLISSC!

The First-Generation and/or Low-Income Student Success Center operates on the pillars of advocacy, mentorship, community & belonging and connections to resources to provide holistic support for first-generation, low-income, transfers, guardian scholars and FLI graduate students at Stanford. This includes providing critical resources, networks, and services that set them up for success.
