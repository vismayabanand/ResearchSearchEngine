Meet Our Team

Welcome the program directory page of our PhD in Societal Computing program. Here, you'll meet the faculty, staff, and students that contribute to our unique academic community. Our team members are deeply involved in research and education, each bringing their own expertise and interests to the table. They are the reason for our program's success and continued excellence in the field of societal computing. We invite you to learn more about them and their work.
