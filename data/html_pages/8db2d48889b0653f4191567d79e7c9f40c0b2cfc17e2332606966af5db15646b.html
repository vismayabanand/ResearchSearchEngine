HSSEAS: Dept Email: [FINAL WEEK TO RSVP] Recruit Undergrads for Your Lab: Spring 2025 Research Fair

Monday, April 21, 2025 11:09:59 AM

Dear Labs and Professors,

The Samueli Undergraduate Research Program is hosting the quarterly research fair on Tuesday, May 13th from 5 PM to 7 PM in the Mong/Patio/Cohen Room and we would love to have representatives from your lab or research group attend. The last day to register for labs is April 29th - please note that this is the final week to RSVP.

This event is a unique opportunity to recruit talented undergraduate engineering students interested in getting involved with your research. At our event held in Fall 2024, we welcomed 450+ students and 25 research labs. The Winter 2024 Research Fair also saw 400+ students and more than 20 labs in attendance. This quarter, we anticipate a similar turnout.

Participating labs will be provided with a booth where up to 3 representatives can talk with students about their research projects and how undergraduate students can get involved.
Don’t miss out on this fantastic opportunity to recruit UCLA engineering students!

If your lab is interested in recruiting undergraduates and attending this event, please fill out this RSVP form ASAP: https://forms.gle/zQWkR1ebWFDpEhNT7

Sign-ups will close on 4/29, so we encourage you to secure your spot promptly. If you know of any other labs or research groups that would be interested in this opportunity, please forward this email to them.

If you have any questions, please email urp@seas.ucla.edu.

Thank you!

Warm Regards,
Office of the Undergraduate Research Program (URP)

Emailed to department by WILLIAM HERRERA (williamh@seas.ucla.edu)
