ACSR Erickson Courts Closure

Due to construction delays, ACSR Courts 1 and 2 have been temporarily converted into indoor tennis courts for the winter quarter to support the varsity tennis program. To maximize the use of these spaces, the courts also feature basketball court lines and four indoor pickleball courts when not in use for varsity tennis.

Stay tuned for more exciting details for unique winter programming that will make the most of these new configurations.
