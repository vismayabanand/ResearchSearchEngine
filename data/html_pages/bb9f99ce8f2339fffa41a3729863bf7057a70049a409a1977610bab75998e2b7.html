SwayBeta.ai

SwayBeta.ai is a research project developed at Carnegie Mellon University by Simon Cullen and Nicholas DiBella is an AI-facilitated group chat platform for student discussions to discuss controversial issues more constructively and help people have better discussions across deep political and moral divides. Find out more at https://www.swaybeta.ai.

Signs of Pink Slime News

Special interest groups are creating thousands of “local” news websites across America (without local reporters) to influence elections. They’re spending millions to get their stories to appear in your newsfeed.

Don’t let these organizations exploit your trust in local news.
