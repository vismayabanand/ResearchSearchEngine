Welcome to the full-text digital archive of Allen Newell's pioneering work in artificial intelligence and cognitive science. Newell earned an international reputation as one of the founders of artificial intelligence. He is also known for his work in the theory of human cognition and the development of computer software and hardware systems for complex information processing.
