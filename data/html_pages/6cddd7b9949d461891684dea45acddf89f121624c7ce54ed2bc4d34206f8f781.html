Pioneering distance learning since 1954.

The Stanford Engineering Center for Global and Online Education (CGOE) partners with Stanford faculty and academic units to develop flexible, credentialed programs that meet the evolving needs of adult learners, industry leaders, and global changemakers, while amplifying faculty research and teaching.

Our Mis­sion

Extend­ing Stan­ford Research and Teach­ing to the World

CGOE has established a scalable, sustainable model of increased access to faculty teaching and research through a mix of outreach initiatives and tuition-based programs. These Stanford programs are delivered online, on campus, and at company locations. They include credit-bearing graduate degree and certificate programs; credentialed professional, enterprise, and custom education programs; and low cost and free and open learning opportunities. 

Sup­port­ing Stan­ford Fac­ul­ty, Depart­ments, Cen­ters and Schools

Within the School of Engineering, CGOE provides support for faculty and departments in the development of new education programs. CGOE’s long history delivering flexible educational experiences to learners around the globe has helped it develop a range of specialized expertise and capabilities. CGOE regularly extends its expertise and support to campus units through synergistic collaboration.

Set­ting the Stan­dard for Non-degree Credentials

CGOE stewards the Stanford Credential Framework which standardizes requirements and rigor across a large portfolio of online and global education programs offering stackable, non-degree credentials. It provides guidelines and assistance university-wide, and issues the majority of non-degree credentials through Stanford Online.
