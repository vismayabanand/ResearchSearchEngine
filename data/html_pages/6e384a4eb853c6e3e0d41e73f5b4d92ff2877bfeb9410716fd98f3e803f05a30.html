Presentation: Breaking through gendered stereotypes with Dr. Laurie Weingart, author of The No Club: Putting a Stop to Womens Dead-End Work

Join Women@SCS for a presentation on breaking through gendered stereotypes in the workplace with Laurie Weingart, Ph.D., co-author of The No Club: Putting a Stop to Women’s Dead-End Work and Richard M. and Margaret S. Cyert Professor of Organizational Behavior and Theory in the Tepper School of Business.

Join us from 5:00 - 6:00 PM on October 9th in CUC Connan (University Center 1st floor) for our workshop on how you can take full control of your professional life.

Reception to follow.

General Events

Biweekly SCS4ALL Meetings

SCS4All meets bi-weekly on Mondays during the fall 2021 semester. SCS4All often organizes different events and activities, so please drop by to learn more about what they're doing!

Tech Nights

Tech Nights is an outreach program run by Women@SCS that hosts bi-annual workshops for middle school girls. Whether you're a student looking to join the program, or if you want to volunteer to help with a workshop, check it out or get in touch to learn more!

Other Events

We regularly have other events scheduled, whether it's a student-faculty lunch, an alumnae panel, or a talk given by upperclassmen. You can find these events scheduled in our calendar above!
