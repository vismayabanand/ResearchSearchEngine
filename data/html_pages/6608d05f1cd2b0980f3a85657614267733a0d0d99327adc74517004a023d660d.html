MyUCR Login

Use this applicant portal, MyUCR, to check the status of your application.

If you’re admitted for fall: Use MyUCR to submit your Statement of Intent to Register (SIR) by May 1 (for first years) and June 1 (for transfers). You will also use MyUCR to register for Highlander Day, April 12 for first years and May 3 for transfers.

If you’re admitted for winter: Use MyUCR to submit your SIR by Oct. 15 and fulfill all required pre-enrollment tasks.

Create Your MyUCR Password (If you don't already have one)

If you applied for admission but have not created a MyUCR password, please locate the email sent to you from UCR Undergraduate Admissions. The email contains log in instructions and a link to create a password. If you have already tried to create a password using that link but were unsuccessful, please contact BearHelp at (951) 827-4848 (IT4U).

Need More Help?

  • If you cannot locate the email, please submit an inquiry.
  • Forgot your username (NetID) or don’t have one? Please submit an inquiry.
  • Have questions? Visit Frequently Asked Questions.
Notice about the Gender Recognition and Lived Name Policy:

Beginning in September 2023, your preferred name will now be the default name that appears when you interact with campus departments. This applies to both you (as a student) and any family you give access to.
