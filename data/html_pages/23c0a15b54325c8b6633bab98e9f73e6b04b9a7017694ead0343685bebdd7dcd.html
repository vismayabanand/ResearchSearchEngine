  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Where can I find information about applying to UCR?
    • If you're an undergraduate, please visit here. 
    • If you're an international student, please visit here. 
    • If you're a transfer student, please visit here. 
  • How much does it cost to go to UCR?

    For more information on tuition and fees, please visit the website of UCR's Office of Financial Aid. 

  • What classes can I take in the Computer Engineering program?

    Computer Engineering students have access to classes in Computer Science and Engineering as well as Computer and Electrical Engineering. Please visit here to explore our course plans and curriculum options. 

  • What research can I do at UCR?

    To discover the various research areas in our Computer Engineering program, please visit here. 

  • Where can I find Computer Engineering internships?

    For information on internship opportunities, visit our college's Student Affairs website. And if you're a current student, make sure you're subscribed to our bi-weekly Career Corner newsletter. If you need to be added, please email careercorner@engr.ucr.edu. 

  • Will I be able to get a job after getting a degree in Computer Engineering from UCR?

    Computer Engineering is an in-demand and growing industry. See the projected job outlook for computer hardware engineers and software engineers from the Bureau of Labor and Statistics. 

  • How much do computer engineers make?

    Median salaries for software and hardware engineers typically range from $105,000 to $117,000, respectively, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. 

  • How can I visit UCR?

    We're excited to welcome you to Bourns College! For campus tour opportunities, please see here.
