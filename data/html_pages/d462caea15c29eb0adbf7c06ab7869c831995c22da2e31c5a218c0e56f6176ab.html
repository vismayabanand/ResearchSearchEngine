OIE "Friday Greetings" Email Newsletter

The Office of International Education greets international students and scholars each Friday with an email newsletter containing upcoming events and activities around the area, as well as quick reminders and announcements, to enhance their CMU experience. We hope that this newsletter will help students and scholars connect with their CMU and local Pittsburgh area community. 

 

Have Content to Share?

If you'd like to submit an event or announcement for publication in the Friday Greetings newsletter, please submit it via our Event Submission Form. OIE and Enrollment Management Communications reserve the right to edit and decline submissions.

Information should be submitted by Wednesday at 5 p.m. EST for inclusion in the following Friday's newsletter.
