Vision and Learning Group

The Vision and Learning Group (formerly Video Computing Group) at the University of California, Riverside conducts research on the foundations and applications of computer vision, image processing, and machine learning.

Principal Investigator: Amit K. Roy-Chowdhury

Current projects are related to visual analysis and understanding, efficient learning and inference, multi-modal data analysis, adversarial attacks/defenses, learning for planning and navigation, distributed machine learning, and applications in physical and biological sciences. The work provides the scientific underpinnings behind applications in cyber-physical, autonomous and intelligent systems. Some of the application areas include environmental monitoring, agriculture, and biology/medicine. Members of the group regularly publish in top-tier conferences and journals in computer vision, machine learning, and image processing. Past members work in major research labs and hold faculty positions across the world.
