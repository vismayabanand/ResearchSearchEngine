Get Involved

Student organizations are a great way to use the skills you learn in the classroom and apply them to real world problems. We encourage all CEN students to enrich their experiences at UCR by actively participating in student clubs. College-wide at the Marlan and Rosemary Bourns College of Engineering, we have more than 20 professional student organizations.
