Social Cybersecurity Seminars

Please join us for the first meeting of the Social Cybersecurity Working Group this semester, where we will be hosting Evan Williams, a fourth-year PhD candidate at Carnegie Mellon University. He will be presenting his research on "Connecting Social Media and Search Engines: Dredge Words and Alt-tech Search Engine Audits.

What: Social Cybersecurity Working Group
When: First Thursday of the month, 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM EST

Remote Details:

Zoom Connectivity: https://renci.zoom.us/j/98870334048
Meeting ID: 988 7033 4048

Evan Williams
PhD student, Carnegie Mellon University
CMU IDeaS Center

Bio:  Evan Williams is a fourth-year PhD Student at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Dr. Kathleen Carley. He formerly worked with the Department of State's Global Engagement Center on efforts to identify international state-backed disinformation campaigns. His research aims to better understand how users access misinformation on search engines. He also works on building models that can help search engines improve the reliability of returned results.
