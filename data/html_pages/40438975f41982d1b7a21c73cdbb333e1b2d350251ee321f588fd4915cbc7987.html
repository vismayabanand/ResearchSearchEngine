Welcome to S3!

Student Support Services (S3) is a friendly and easily accessible support hub for undergraduate students. Whether you're struggling with a PSET due to personal issues, feeling too sick to take an exam, considering taking time away from the Institute, or just aren't sure who to talk to, we can help. S^3 is a private resource.

Our office is closed Monday, April 21st due to the holiday.

Are you a graduate student? Please visit our colleagues in GradSupport for support resources.

Are you faculty or staff? For non-urgent or academic guidance about an undergraduate student, please email s3-support@mit.edu or call 617-253-4861. For more urgent concerns, visit DoingWell at MIT.
