Sponsorship

We welcome industry sponsors to join us in supporting Women@SCS as we work to engage, and sustain a strong cohort of women in the field.

Women@SCS  ›  Get Involved

Women@SCS has a tradition of working closely with industry and research groups ot both understand critical gender issues in professional settings, as well as exploring job opportunities for women in computing related domains.

The three primary value propositions for sponsors are:

  • Taking a high-profile leadership position in the global effort to recognize and encourage women in computer science
  • Direct connection to a pool of top talent in the field, including access to student resumes (with their permission)
  • Sending a strong message about diversity among your company values
If you're interested in sponsoring Women@SCS, please contact Jodi Forlizzi
