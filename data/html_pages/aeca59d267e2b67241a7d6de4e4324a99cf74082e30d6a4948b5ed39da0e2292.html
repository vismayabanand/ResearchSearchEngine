Value of a UC Riverside Degree

Invest In Your Future. Get a UC Riverside Degree.

Enjoy more earnings, career options, and employability as a Highlander! You’ll be part of one of the most diverse student bodies at a top-tier research university in the United States and have a home within the University of California system, one of the world’s premier public universities. Explore outcomes shared by faculty and alumni who are making bold and brilliant impacts in their professions and communities.

Top-Tier Research University

Graduate with skills and experiences desired by employers.

UC Riverside is a proud member of the prestigious Association of American Universities (AAU), alongside institutions like Harvard, Stanford, UC Berkeley, and UCLA. UCR is also the most affordable AAU in Southern California!

A Best Value College

Based on return on investment including academics, affordability, and career outcomes for graduates.

Top 1.3% of Universities Worldwide

Based on those who applied for aid.

Career Guidance

Build Your Network

As a UCR Highlander, you’ll build a broad network of mentors and colleagues with faculty, staff, and students. After graduation, you’ll have access to the UCR Alumni Association, a network of more than 143,000 Highlanders with annual events and resources to help you find your first job after graduation!
