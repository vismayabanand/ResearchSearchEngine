Request Repairs

MIT Department of Facilities

Order Services

To request building repairs or installations go to Service Requests in Atlas. There is no charge for Basic Services but there is for Special Services.Â  If you require services other than "Basic," or if you do not have access to the Atlas system, please contact your Faculty Host's Administrative Assistant.

Basic Services

There is no charge for repairs or adjustments to building systems such as:

  • Lights, shades and windows
  • Domestic water, process water and drainage systems
  • Blackboards, doors, elevators, floors, ceilings
  • Roofs
  • Lab fume hoods
  • Building clocks
  • Electrical, piping, mechanical or structural systems
  • HVAC systems
  • Piping systems
  • Other repairs as needed
