To be added to the distribution list send email to: casos[at]cmu.edu

Dynamics meetings are open to any student or staff interested in the speaker's topic or CASOS and network analysis in general. If you plan on attending to a future meeting, please let the CASOS department know by email so that sufficient space is reserved.
