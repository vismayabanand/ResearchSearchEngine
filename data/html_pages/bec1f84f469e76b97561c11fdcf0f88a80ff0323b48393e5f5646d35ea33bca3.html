We have assembled a downloadable casebook template. This template should be helpful in understanding the sort of materials required by the review committees.

Tenure Decision Document (TDD)

Need to know your Tenure Schedule?

When you start as a tenure stream faculty member, you are required to complete a TDD. Any adjustments to your tenure date require a revised version. Download the Word document format to customize the TDD, or the standard form in PDF format.
